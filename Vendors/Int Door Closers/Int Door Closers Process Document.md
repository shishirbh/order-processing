# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > International Door Closers vendor orders are processed via email using Backoffice only.

  - > Every order must be sent via email using the correct template

  - > Vendor acknowledgment must be received before proceeding with the order

  - > If No Acknowledgment **→** Follow up after 24 hrs

  - > FedEx usage is not acceptable for this vendor

  - > No internal notes may appear in vendor emails

  - > Correct subject line must match shipping type

  - > Stock cannot be checked via the website, but you can always refer to recent orders to see if there are any issues (e.g., discontinued items, ETAs, etc.)

  - > Items over 32 inches OR over 10 lbs must be dropshipped

  - > Standard ETA is \~7 business days

### **Always Avoid – Critical Traps**

❌ Never send expedited order without approval

❌ Never send emails with internal notes

❌ Never change subject lines incorrectly

❌ Never mix templates

❌ Never assume acknowledgment — must wait

❌ Never send to wrong vendor email

❌ Never use old customer addresses from past orders

❌ Never include customer phone numbers incorrectly formatted

### **1.2 Email Rules (Strict Compliance)**

✔ Use only approved templates  
✔ Subject line must match shipping type  
✔ Include expedited text only when applicable  
✔ Remove all internal product notes  
✔ Use correct customer address (no old data)

❌ No internal comments  
❌ No template mixing  
❌ No manual re-wording  
❌ No wrong vendor email

## **2.2 Dropship – Next Day or Second Day (Expedited)**

**Approval Required – Mandatory**

Before Sending Any Expedited Email:

  - > Check with Elina

  - > No approval = **DO NOT SEND**

**If Approved:**

  - > Use correct subject line

  - > Repeat shipping instruction **twice** in body

  - > Never modify wording

**2.2.1 Next Day Shipping Template:**

**Subject:** NEXT DAY – Dropship Order

Hi,

Please process a new order attached.

PLEASE SHIP NEXT DAY

PLEASE SHIP NEXT DAY

Let us know if any changes.

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

**2.2.2 Second Day Shipping Template**

**Subject:** SECOND DAY – Dropship Order

Hi,

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 INTERNAL PROCESSING STEPS (for In-House and Dropship Orders)**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Verify & Move Order to Submit**

### Before submitting, confirm:

  - > Pricing

  - > Quantities

  - > Shipping type

  - > Customer address (dropship)

  - > Remove internal notes

  - > Check recent order history for issues

### Then → Move vendor order to SUBMIT.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Process Through Email**

  - ### In Backoffice:

  - ### Click By Email

  - ### Select correct shipping template

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Insert Correct Draft Template**

### Based on shipping type:

  - ### Standard In-House  
    → Use In-House Standard draft.

  - ### Dropship – Standard Ground  
    → Use the Dropship Standard draft.

  - ### Dropship – NEXT DAY / SECOND DAY  
    → Must include BOTH:

<!-- end list -->

  - ### Correct subject line

  - ### Shipping instruction lines:

<!-- end list -->

  - ### SHIP CUSTOMER NEXT DAY

  - ### SHIP CUSTOMER SECOND DAY

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Final Validation**

Confirm:

  - > Correct subject line

  - > Correct shipping instructions

  - > No internal notes

  - > Correct address

  - > Correct email signature

### **3.1.5 Step 5 Vendor Response**

  - > Wait for acknowledgment**  
    > **

  - > If no response in 24 hours:
    
      - > Resend with subject:  
        > **FOLLOW UP – Awaiting Acknowledgment**
