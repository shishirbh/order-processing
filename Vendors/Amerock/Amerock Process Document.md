# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Amerock vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

<!-- end list -->

  - > **Ordering Method:** Vendor Website – [**<span class="underline">amerockgo.com</span>**](http://amerockgo.com)

  - > **Ordering Access:** Login using assigned vendor key

<!-- end list -->

  - > Dropship allowed only for items priced above $50 AND in stock

  - > In-House Orders required for all items $50 or below

  - > Backorders are allowed and processed without contacting vendor

  - > Blind Shipping is not applicable (no dropship for items under $50)

**Note:** Product detail pages may appear blank or limited — this is normal. Always rely on the **Stock Availability tab**, not product pages.

# **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Never dropship items priced $50 or below  
❌ Never skip stock check  
❌ Never contact Amerock for backorders  
❌ Never submit an order without PO number  
❌ Never assume ETA is guaranteed

### **1.2 In-House and Dropship Eligibility Rules**

**1.2.1 Minimum Order Requirement:**

  - > $50 minimum applies ONLY to in-house vendor orders

  - > Dropship orders do not require a minimum, but item price must be above $50

  - > YES THE MINIMUM for any dropship order is $50.00

**1.2.2 In-House Order Rules:**

  - > Items priced $50 or below must ship to DK Hardware warehouse

  - > Order total must be ≥ $50

  - > DK Hardware address must be selected at checkout

**1.2.3 Dropship Rules (Strict):**

Dropship is allowed ONLY IF ALL conditions below are met:

  - > Item price \> $50

  - > Item shows available stock

  - > Customer address is entered manually

  - > Shipping method is selected correctly

Strictly Avoid:

❌ Never dropship items priced $50 or below  
❌ Never dropship out-of-stock items

**1.3 Backorder Rules (Very Important)**

If stock shows:

  - > **0 units**, OR

  - > **“Please contact customer service”**

➡ **Process the order normally as a backorder**

❌ Do **NOT** email or call Amerock  
❌ Do **NOT** hold the order  
❌ Do **NOT** cancel unless instructed

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 —** Log into amerockgo.com

**2.1.2 Step 2 —** Open Stock Availability tab  
  
**2.1.3 Step 3 —** Paste item code  
  
**2.1.4 Step 4 —** Click Check Availability

**2.1.5 Step 5 —** Review stock status

**2.1.6 Step 6 —** Enter quantity

**2.1.7 Step 7 —** Click Add to Cart

### **2.1.8 Step 8 —** Open Cart

### **2.1.9 Step 9 —** Confirm total ≥ $50

### **2.1.10 Step 10 —** Click Checkout

### **2.1.11 Step 11 —** Select DK Hardware Address

### **2.1.12 Step 12 —** Paste PO Number

### **2.1.13 Step 13 —** Verify:

  - > Items

  - > Quantities

  - > Address

### **2.1.14 Step 14 —** Click Place Order

**Example:**

  - > Item price: $18

  - > Quantity: 4

  - > Total: $72 → ✅ Valid in-house order

# **2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 —** Log into amerockgo.com

**2.2.2 Step 2 —** Open Stock Availability tab  
  
**2.2.3 Step 3 —** Paste item code  
  
**2.2.4 Step 4 —** Click Check Availability

**2.2.5 Step 5 —** Review stock status

**2.2.6 Step 6 —** Enter quantity

**2.2.7 Step 7 —** Add items (only items \> $50 and in stock)

**2.2.8 Step 8 —** Click Checkout

**2.2.9 Step 9 —** Manually enter customer shipping address

**2.2.9 Step 10 —** Select shipping method:

  - > Next Day → If website allows

  - > 2 Business Day → Only if item is available in local stock

**2.2.11 Step 11 —** Paste PO Number  
→ Double-check address & shipping  
  
**2.2.12 Step 12 —** Click Place Order

**Example:**

  - > Item price: $75

  - > Stock: Available

  - > Shipping: Next Day → ✅ Valid dropship
