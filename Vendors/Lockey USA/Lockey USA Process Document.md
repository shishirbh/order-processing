# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > All Lockey USA orders are processed via Backoffice → By Email

  - > No minimum order value

  - > Use the correct draft template — no free-typing

  - > Remove ALL product notes below the item description before sending

  - > Customer orders are dropshipped only using DK Hardware FEDEX account \#632647611

  - > Expedited orders MUST use DK Hardware FEDEX account \#632647611

  - > Never include invoice or packing slip for customer orders

  - > Heavy items MAY be dropshipped (verify before processing)

  - > If dropship order value \< $100 → apply 10% surcharge

  - > ETA Standard Ground - 7 days

## **Always Avoid:**

> ❌ Including invoices or packing slips  
> ❌ Forgetting FEDEX lines on dropship/ expedite orders  
> ❌ Sending orders with internal notes  
> ❌ Using wrong template  
> ❌ Missing 10% surcharge on \<$100 dropship  
> ❌ Processing international customer orders  
> ❌ Do NOT leave customer orders in incorrect status  
> ❌ Do NOT process expedited orders as ground

### **1.2 Order Types**

**1.2.1 In-House Orders (before this add, Local stock orders-** DK Hardware stock / internal replenishment)

  - > No minimum order value

  - > Ground shipping only

  - > Standard vendor draft

**1.2.2 Dropship Orders - Standard Ground**

  - > Ships directly to customer

  - > No minimum order value

  - > Dropship required

  - > Apply 10% surcharge if order \< $100

  - > Special FEDEX account lines required (mandatory)

**1.2.3 Dropship Orders - Expedited (1-Day / 2-Day)**

  - > Dropship only

  - > Uses DK Hardware FEDEX account

  - > NEXT DAY or SECOND DAY only

  - > Special FEDEX account lines required (mandatory)

## **1.3 Minimum Order & Surcharge Rules**

### **Vendor Orders (In-House):**

  - > No minimum

  - > Standard draft only

  - > Confirm discontinued items if applicable

### **Customer Dropship Orders:**

  - > No minimum

  - > If total \< $100 → add 10% surcharge

  - > Heavy items: verify dropship eligibility before sending

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 INTERNAL PROCESSING STEPS - ALL ORDERS**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Verify & Move Order to Submit**

### Before submitting, confirm:

  - ### Pricing correct

  - ### Quantities accurate

  - ### Shipping method selected

  - > 10% surcharge added to orders less than $100

### Then → Move vendor order to SUBMIT.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Process Through Email**

  - ### In Backoffice:

  - ### Click By Email

  - ### Select correct shipping template

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Insert Correct Draft Template**

### Based on shipping type:

  - ### Standard In-House  
    → Use In-House Standard draft.

  - ### Dropship – Standard Ground  
    → Use the Dropship Standard draft.

  - ### Dropship – NEXT DAY / SECOND DAY  
    → Must include BOTH:

<!-- end list -->

  - ### Correct subject line

  - ### Shipping instruction lines:

<!-- end list -->

  - ### NEXT DAY – Customer Dropship Order

  - ### SECOND DAY – Customer Dropship Order

#### **For Expedited Orders ONLY:**

  - > Insert FEDEX ACCOUNT lines TWICE

  - > Ensure service matches subject line (NEXT / SECOND DAY)

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Final Checklist**

### Before hitting send, confirm:

❏ No internal product notes  
❏ Correct template used  
❏ Correct FEDEX lines added (if expedited)  
❏ Shipping address matches customer request  
❏ 10% surcharge applied if dropship \< $100  
❏ NO invoice or packing slip included

🚫 If even ONE item fails → DO NOT SEND
