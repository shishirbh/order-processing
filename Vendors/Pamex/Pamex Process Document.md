# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > All orders are processed by email only via Backoffice

  - > Correct email template must be used based on shipping type

  - > Vendor acknowledgment is mandatory before moving forward

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV): $1,000

  - > No internal notes may appear in vendor emails

  - > Expedited shipping requires explicit shipping instructions

  - > FedEx Account \#632647611 must be used for all expedited/dropship shipments

## **Always Avoid:**

❌ Sending order without vendor acknowledgment  
❌ Including internal notes  
❌ Missing shipping instructions  
❌ Using wrong email template  
❌ Assuming minimum order blocks processing

### **1.2 Order Types & Handling Rules**

**1.2.1 Vendor Order — In-House (Standard Processing)**

**When Used:**

  - > Stock replenishment

  - > Orders shipping to DK Hardware (not customer)

**Rules:**

  - > Processed via email

  - > Must meet $1,000 minimum

  - > If below $1,000, order may still proceed using DK FedEx account

  - > Vendor acknowledgment required before order is considered placed

**Example:**

> Order total = $850  
> ✅ Allowed  
> ✅ Use DK FedEx account  
> ❌ Do NOT cancel or delay solely due to minimum

### **1.2.2 Customer Order — Standard Ground**

**Rules:**

  - > Convert to In-House Vendor Order

  - > Process exactly like standard vendor order

  - > No expedited language needed

  - > Minimum rules apply

**Example:**

> Customer chooses ground shipping  
> → Treat as normal vendor order  
> → Use standard email template

### **1.2.2 Customer Order — 1–2 Business Day (Expedited)**

**Critical Rule:  
**🚨 Make sure dimensions and weight are updated

❌ Never assume expedited approval

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 INTERNAL PROCESSING STEPS (for In-House and Dropship Orders)**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Pre-Check**

Verify:

  - > Pricing accuracy

  - > Quantity correctness

  - > Shipping method

  - > Minimum order handling

### Then → change vendor order to SUBMIT status.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Process Through Email**

In Backoffice:

  - > Click By Email

  - > Select correct draft:
    
      - > Standard
    
      - > Next Day
    
      - > Second Day

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Insert Correct Draft Template**

  - > Standard → Standard template only

  - > **Next / Second Day** →
    
      - > Correct subject
    
      - > Required shipping line included twice (as per vendor requirement)

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Final Validation**

  - > Confirm:  
    > ✔ No internal notes  
    > ✔ Correct FedEx account (dropship/expedite)  
    > ✔ Minimum handled correctly  
    > ✔ Shipping method clearly stated

Only then → **Send Email**

### **3.1.5 Step 5 Vendor Response**

  - > Wait for acknowledgment**  
    > **

  - > If no response in 24 hours:
    
      - > Resend with subject:  
        > → **FOLLOW UP – Awaiting Acknowledgment**
