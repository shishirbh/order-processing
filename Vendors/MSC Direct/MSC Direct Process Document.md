# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 General Rules**

  - > Alternate Vendor: ORS Nasco

  - > Order Minimum: $250 (strict) for BOTH In-House and Dropship

  - > Backorders Allowed: Yes

  - > Price Validity: Only for available quantity (strict compliance required)

<!-- end list -->

  - > If vendor shows limited stock → do NOT exceed vendor’s available quantity.

  - > If vendor shows “Ships from Manufacturer” / “Contact Us” / “Special Item” → Vendor Quote always required.

  - > Customization → Vendor Quote required. Signed Customer Quote is only required for special/modified items or large quantity/high-value orders — not for routine customizations. (Updated: 2026-04-26)

  - > Sales must forward email trail to Purchasing.

**1.2 Mandatory Quote Rules**

### **Quotes ALWAYS Required When:**

  - > Ships from Manufacturer

  - > We can’t sell online

  - > Special Item

  - > Customization

  - > Freight OFS

  - > Price discrepancy

  - > Order quantity \> vendor stock

### **Quote Package Must Include:**

From Sales:

  - > Email trail

  - > PDF Quote

  - > Customer signed quote (only if special/modified item or large qty/high-value order) (Updated: 2026-04-26)

### **1.3 Stock Quantity Rule**

MSC & ORS show stock-based pricing. Apply this rule based on the item type: (Updated: 2026-04-26)

**When price is listed for available stock only (stock-limited pricing):**

**✔** Check vendor stock every time  
**✔** Do NOT exceed the available quantity shown — the price applies only to that qty  
**✔** Take a vendor quote if customer needs more than the listed stock  
**✔** Add internal note: “Vendor qty limited — quote required above X qty”

**When item is a regular item (standard pricing):** (Updated: 2026-04-26)

**✔** You may order more than the displayed stock — vendor will place remaining units on backorder  
**✔** Backorders are allowed for regular items

### **Do NOT:**

❌ Ignore stock-limited pricing warnings  
❌ Assume vendor honors stock-limited price for a higher quantity

This is a critical violation area.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

## **2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Item**

Check vendor item on MSC Direct website:

  - > Image

  - > Description

  - > Specs

  - > Availability

  - > Price

  - > Stock quantity — apply stock-limited rule only when pricing is for available qty; regular items allow backorder (Updated: 2026-04-26)

**Cross-verify on DKHardware.com:**

  - > Same image

  - > Same spec

  - > Same price (or price discrepancy workflow)

### **If Price Does Not Match DK Site**

  - > Share price with Elina & Kirill

  - > Back office updates pricing & informs customer

  - > Only after confirmation → proceed

## **2.1.2 Step 2 — Add to Cart**

  - > Add items to cart (even if out of stock — backorders allowed)

## **2.1.3 Step 3 — Check Item Notes**

  - > If any of the following appears:

Table 1 - Item Notes and Required Action

| **Vendor Message**      | **Required Action**         |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Ships from Manufacturer | Take vendor quote           |
| We can’t sell online    | Take vendor quote           |
| Special Item            | Take vendor quote           |
| Customization           | Vendor quote + Signed quote |

❌ **Do NOT place order until quote is attached.**

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Verify Cart Total**

  - > Cart must be ≥ $250

  - > If below → HOLD and combine with other items

  - > Add internal note: “Awaiting combine to reach MSC minimum”

**2.1.5 Step 5 — Checkout (In-House)**

  - > Click Secure Checkout

  - > Shipping Address: Pre-saved DKH Warehouse

  - > Attention: DK Hardware Supply

  - > Payment Method: Bill to MSC Account

  - > Enter PO number

  - > Submit order

  - > ETA: 7 days unless quoted otherwise.

# **2.2 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **Applies ONLY when:**

  - > Customer Order is Dropship

  - > Item is NOT Special Order (SP)/Out-of-Stock (OFS) requiring quote

  - > Vendor minimum ($250) is met

## **Steps:**

**2.2.1 Step 1 —** Create a Dropship Vendor Order.  
**2.2.2 Step 2 —** Verify item (same as in-house)  
**2.2.3 Step 3 —** Add item(s) to cart  
**2.2.4 Step 4 —** Click Secure Checkout

**2.2.5 Step 5 —** Update the shipping address to the customer's address.  
**2.2.6 Step 6 —** In the Attention field → Enter the Customer’s Name.  
**2.2.7 Step 7 —** Payment Method→ Bill to MSC Account  
**2.2.8 Step 8 —** Enter PO Number → Submit  
**2.2.9 Step 9 —** ETA: 7 days.

# **2.3 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **2.3.1 Step 1 — Validate Customer Information**

Use:

  - > Google

  - > Whitepages

  - > Credit Card Verification  
    > Proceed ONLY if:  
    > ✔ Name  
    > ✔ Address  
    > ✔ Phone  
    > ✔ Card  
    > → Should match

## **2.3.2 Step 2 — Verify Item Details**

Cross-check DK site vs MSC portal:

  - > Image

  - > Specs

  - > Price

  - > Description

  - > Availability

## **2.3.3 Step 3 — SO / OFS Rules**

### **SO (Special Order)**

  - > Customization → Signed quote needed

  - > Vendor quote ALWAYS needed

  - > Sales must forward email trail

### **OFS (Out of Stock)**

  - > Regular OFS → No quote needed

  - > Freight OFS → Vendor quote required

## **2.3.4 Step 4 — Process Incoming Order**

  - > Open Process Incoming Order page.

## **2.3.5 Step 5 — Assign Products**

### **If Local Stock available**

→ Assign from local

### **If Local Stock NOT available**

→ Assign full quantity from MSC

### **Status Outcomes**

  - > 100% from MSC → Coming In-House

  - > Partial Local + Partial MSC → In-House with Backorder

## **2.3.6 Step 6 — Create Vendor Order**

  - > Use existing VO OR

  - > Create new VO (In-House)

## **2.3.7 Step 7 — Payment**

  - > Authorize & charge

**EXCEPT:**

  - > Amazon

  - > Zoro

  - > Net30 customers

## **2.3.8 Step 8 — Finalize**

  - > Click **Process Changes**

  - > VO number is generated

**2.4 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **2.4.1 Step 1 — Customer Verification**

  - > Same validation as In-House.

### **2.4.2 Step 2 — Item Verification**

Same steps as In-House:

  - > Image

  - > Specs

  - > Price

  - > Availability

  - > Stock limit (strict rule applies)

### **2.4.3 Step 3 — SO / OFS Handling**

If item is:

  - > SO

  - > OFS

  - > Special Item

  - > Ships from Manufacturer  
    > → Vendor Quote Required

Customization → Signed quote required.

### **2.4.4 Step 4 — Dropship Eligibility**

  - > Freight? → Confirm with Marina/Elina before dropshipping

  - > Zoro orders → Never dropship

  - > Amazon/eBay orders → Never dropship unless freight exception approved

### **2.4.5 Step 5 — Assign Products**

Always assign **100% from MSC** (local stock is NEVER used)

### **Status**

  - > Dropship → Placed for Dropship

  - > Color: Red → Green after assignment

### **2.4.6 Step 6 — Create Vendor Order**

  - > Always New VO

  - > Select Dropship Option

### **2.4.7 Step 7 — Payment**

  - > Authorize & charge  
    > **EXCEPT Amazon / Zoro / Net30**

### **2.4.8 Step 8 — Finalize**

  - > Click Process Changes

  - > VO number generated
