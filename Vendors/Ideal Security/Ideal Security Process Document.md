# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Perfect Score Technologies vendor orders are processed through emails via Backoffice (BO).

  - > Minimum Order Value is $400

  - > Vendor acknowledgment required before final processing

  - > Shipping Method is Standard Ground via FedEx ACC \#632647611 only

  - > No dropshipping under any circumstances

## **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Processing orders below $400  
❌ Dropshipping any Ideal Security order  
❌ Using wrong subject lines or templates  
❌ Including internal notes in vendor emails  
❌ Assuming acknowledgment without confirmation  
❌ Using any FedEx account other than \#632647611**  
** ❌ Sending orders to the wrong vendor email

### **1.2 ORDER TYPES & RULES**

**1.2.1 Vendor - In-House Orders**

  - > **Purpose:** Replenish DK Hardware warehouse stock

  - > **Shipping Method:** Standard Ground (FedEx)

  - > **Conditions to Proceed:**
    
      - > Order total ≥ $400
    
      - > Email sent using approved template
    
      - > Vendor acknowledgment received

**Mandatory Email Instructions:**

  - > Must explicitly state:
    
      - > “PLEASE SHIP USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611”

  - > No internal comments or notes allowed

**1.2.2 Customer Order (NON-DROPSHIP)**

  - > **Shipping:** Standard Ground only

  - > **Dropshipping:** ❌ Not allowed

  - > **Stock Rule:**
    
      - > Orders can be processed only if item is available in local warehouse stock

**If Item Is Out of Stock:**

  - > Do **not** process

  - > Notify customer immediately

  - > Confirm shipment timing once stock is available

### **1.3 Edge Case Handling (MANDATORY)**

#### **1.3.1 Order Below $400**

  - > ❌ Do not process

  - > Options:
    
      - > Add additional items **OR**
    
      - > Escalate to supervisor

**1.3.2 Item Out of Stock (Customer Order)**

  - > Do not submit

  - > Notify customer

  - > Confirm shipment timing once replenished

**1.3.3 Incorrect or Incomplete Address**

  - > Stop processing

  - > Contact customer for verification

  - > Resume only after confirmation

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR and CUSTOMER ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Verify & Move Order to Submit**

### Before submitting, confirm:

  - ### Pricing correct

  - ### Quantities accurate

  - > Confirm shipping method = Standard Ground

  - > Confirm order total ≥ $400

  - ### No restricted notes

### Then → Move vendor order to SUBMIT.

❌ If any check fails → **STOP**

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Stock Availability Check**

  - > **Vendor Orders:** Proceed once MOV is met

  - > **Customer Orders:**
    
      - > Process only if item is in local warehouse stock

❌ Out of stock → Notify customer, do not submit

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Email Preparation**

  - > Select **Standard Ground** template only

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Correct subject line
    
      - > Correct FedEx account number
    
      - > Correct shipping address

  - > Remove **all internal notes**

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Send Email & Wait**

  - > Send order email to correct Ideal Security contact

  - > Do not process further without acknowledgment

**3.1.5 Step 5 — No acknowledgment after 24 hours**

  - > Resend email with subject:  
    > **“FOLLOW UP – Awaiting Acknowledgment”**
