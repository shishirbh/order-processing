# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

  - > Gaab Locks LLC orders are processed via email using Backoffice (BO).

  - > All orders are dropship ONLY.

  - > Vendor Email: <span class="underline">po@gaablocks.com</span>

  - > DK Hardware always provides the shipping label.

  - > Vendor never ships using their own carrier account.

  - > Dropship procedures vary based on:  
    > → item size  
    > → order quantity  
    > → urgency (standard vs expedited)

<!-- end list -->

  - > Always monitor inbox after sending requests for:  
    > → dimension confirmations  
    > → shipping updates  
    > → vendor questions

## **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Generating shipping labels without dimensions  
❌ Creating PO before shipping approval (large orders)  
❌ Allowing vendor to use their own shipping account  
❌ Assuming large orders are parcel-eligible  
❌ Skipping Marina verification for bulk shipments

**1.2 ORDER CLASSIFICATION (MANDATORY FIRST STEP)**

Every Gaab Locks order **must be classified before processing**.

Evaluate the order based on:

  - > Item size

  - > Quantity

  - > Urgency (standard vs expedited)

This classification determines **how much verification is required** before PO creation.

## **1.3 EDGE CASES & OPERATIONAL SAFEGUARDS**

### **1.3.1 Vendor Delays in Providing Dimensions**

  - > Order stays **ON HOLD**

  - > Do not proceed without data

### **1.3.2 Dimensions Indicate Freight After Review**

  - > Stop processing

  - > Re-confirm shipping method with Marina

### **1.3.3 Vendor Attempts to Use Their Own Shipping**

❌ Not allowed  
➡️ Always reiterate DK Hardware label policy

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING SMALL ITEMS / SMALL QUANTITY ORDERS - DROPSHIP**

**Definition:**

Any order that is -

  - > Small physical size

  - > Low quantity

  - > Non-freight

  - > Typically FedEx-eligible

#### **Process Overview for small items or low quantities:**

  - > Dropship can be processed without checking stock availability or dimensions upfront.

  - > Because DK Hardware generates shipping labels, the following is mandatory:  
    > → Package weight  
    > → Package dimensions

  - > Vendor must be notified during processing.

**STEPS:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 —** **Send dropship request to vendor**

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Request package weight & dimensions**

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Wait for vendor confirmation**

  - > Request Marina to generate shipping label.

  - > Share label with vendor.

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Request Marina to generate shipping label**

**2.1.5 Step 5 — Share shipping label with vendor**

**2.1.6 Step 6 — Vendor ships directly to customer**

**2.1.7 Vendor Email Template – Small Items**

Hello,

Please process this order.

Kindly provide the package weight and dimensions so that we may generate and share our shipping label.

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

**Example:**

  - > Order: 2 door lock cylinders

  - > Vendor confirms: 5 lbs, 10×8×6 box

  - > Marina generates FedEx label

  - > Label shared → Vendor ships

# **2.2 PROCESSING LARGE ORDERS / LARGE QUANTITY ORDERS - DROPSHIP**

### **Definition:**

Any order that is -

  - > Bulky

  - > Heavy

  - > High quantity

  - > Potentially freight-eligible

For large items or bulk quantities, **additional verification is required** before processing.

**2.2.1 Required Confirmations:**

  - > Overall shipping dimensions

  - > Total shipment weight

  - > Shipping method confirmation:
    
      - > Palletized
    
      - > Non-palletized

  - > Determine whether the shipment:
    
      - > Can be handled via FedEx
    
      - > Requires LTL freight

This verification helps prevent unexpected freight charges or shipping method issues.

## **2.2.2 Shipping Cost Verification**

After receiving shipping dimensions and weight from the vendor:

  - > Shipping costs must be **verified through Marina**

  - > We ship using **our own shipping account**

**Example:**

Vendor Address for Shipping Quotes -

7680 NW 78 Terrace  
Medley, FL 33166

→ Marina will review the **shipping feasibility and cost** based on the provided information.

Shipping is either:

> ✅ Approved or
> 
> ❌ Rejected / Needs re-evaluation
