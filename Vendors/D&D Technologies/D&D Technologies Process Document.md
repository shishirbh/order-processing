# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > D\&D Technologies vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

All orders must follow:

  - > Vendor communication rules

  - > There is no minimum order value requirement

  - > Correct shipping-type email templates

  - > Mandatory vendor acknowledgment

### **Key Rule:**

➡ ️ No order is considered processed until acknowledgment is received from the vendor.

# **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Sending expedited orders without Elina’s approval  
❌ Sending emails containing internal notes  
❌ Using incorrect subject lines  
❌ Mixing templates  
❌ Assuming acknowledgment — always wait  
❌ Sending to incorrect vendor email  
❌ Using outdated customer addresses  
❌ Formatting customer phone numbers incorrectly

### **1.2 Order Categories**

**1.2.1 In-House Orders**

Use In-House processing when:

  - > DK warehouse needs stock

  - > Order is replenishment

  - > Customer order must be fulfilled via warehouse (not dropship)

  - > Shipping method = Standard Ground only

**1.2.2 Dropship – Standard Ground**

Use when:

  - > Customer selected normal shipping

  - > No expedited service requested

  - > No special instructions

**1.2.3 Dropship – Expedited (1–2 Business Day)**

Expedited orders must ALWAYS be approved by Elina before sending.

Two Types:

1.  > Second Day Shipping

2.  > Next Day Shipping

### **4.2.1 Mandatory Rules:**

  - > Must check with Elina before sending ANY expedited request.

  - > Email must include shipping instructions **TWICE** in the body.

  - > Subject line must clearly reflect the shipping type.

  - > Customer address must be double-checked.

  - > Internal notes must be removed.

**  
1.3 Edge Cases**

### **1.3.1 Case 1 — Customer asked for Next Day but lives in remote area**

→ Ask Elina → If approval granted → Send Next Day template  
→ If not → Use Standard Ground

### **1.3.2 Case 2 — Order is below minimum value**

→ Add another needed item OR  
→ Escalate to supervisor if customer-specific necessity

### **1.3.3 Case 3 — Customer address incomplete**

→ Contact customer immediately  
→ Do NOT send until address is verified

### **1.3.4 Case 4 — Vendor changes price**

→ Update BO  
→ Confirm with customer (if applicable)

### **1.3.5 Case 5 — Customer wants to upgrade shipping after placing order**

→ Follow expedited rules (check with Elina)

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 INTERNAL PROCESSING RULES (All Orders)**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Step 1 — Verification**

Check:

  - > Pricing

  - > Quantities

  - > Customer address (for dropship)

  - > Minimum order requirement

  - > Any vendor special instructions

  - > Remove internal notes

### **2.1.2 Step 2 — Move to Submit**

Move order to **Submit** in BO.

### **2.1.3 Step 3 — Email Sending Procedure**

In BO:  
→ Click **By Email  
** → Choose correct template:

  - > Standard

  - > Dropship Standard

  - > Next Day

  - > Second Day

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Final Checks**

Must ensure:

  - > Correct template

  - > Correct subject

  - > Correct shipping instructions

  - > No internal notes

  - > Email signature correct

  - > Vendor email correct

  - > Attachment included

### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Vendor Response**

  - > Wait for acknowledgment

  - > If no response within **24 hours**, resend email with the subject:  
    > **"FOLLOW UP – Awaiting Acknowledgment"**

# **2.2 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify Requirements**

  - > Check minimum order value

  - > Confirm stock availability

  - > Remove internal notes

  - > Confirm correct warehouse ship-to address

**2.2.2 Step 2 — Move Order to “Submit” in BO**

**2.2.3 Step 3 — Click** **"By Email"**

**2.2.4 Step 4 — Insert Standard Template  
**  
**2.2.5 Step 5 — Perform Final Checks:**

  - > Correct subject: “Please Process Order”

  - > No internal notes

  - > Attachment present

  - > Vendor email correct

**2.2.6 Step 6 — Send Email**

**2.2.7 Step 7 — Wait for acknowledgement**

**2.2.8 Step 8 — Once acknowledgment received → mark order as confirmed**

**2.2.9 Step 9 — Standard Email Template**

**Subject:** **Please Process Order**

Hi,

Kindly process the order. Please let me know in case of any changes.

Thanks and Regards,

Purchasing Team

**2.3.2 Step 2 — Select Standard Ground Dropship Template**
