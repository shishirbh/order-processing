# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

  - > Exact Replacement Parts, Inc. (ERP) vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > Order Type is In-House ONLY - Dropship strictly prohibited.

  - > MOV Requirement: Orders must meet a minimum order value of $1000 to be processed.

  - > Request Elina to add items to meet the order minimum for coming in-house vendor order.

  - > Shipping Method for all orders is Standard Ground (via vendor's carrier) only.

  - > Expedited orders are not supported unless the item is available in DK Hardware warehouse stock.

### **Always Avoid:**

❌ Processing orders below $1,000  
❌ Processing discontinued items  
❌ Assuming expedited shipping  
❌ Attempting dropship  
❌ Skipping oversize charge verification and customer confirmation  
❌ Promising delivery timelines without stock confirmation

**1.2 Expedited Orders (1-2 Business Day Shipping)**

  - > **Ground Shipping Only**: All orders, including expedited ones, will be shipped via Standard Ground unless the item is available in our local warehouse.

  - > **If Local Stock is Available**: If the item is in our warehouse, we will process it for expedited shipping. Otherwise, the customer will be informed that Ground shipping will be used.

#### **1.2.1 Process for Expedited Orders:**

  - > **Check Warehouse Stock**: Confirm if the item is available in our local warehouse stock.

  - > **If Available in Warehouse**: Proceed with expedited shipping.

  - > **If Not Available in Warehouse**: Notify the customer that the order will ship Ground.

**1.3 Handling Discontinued Items**

#### **1.3.1 Identifying Discontinued Items:**

  - > **Unavailable Indicator**: Discontinued items are clearly marked as unavailable in red text when you open the item page on the vendor’s website.

#### **1.3.2 Handling Discontinued Items:**

  - > **Do Not Process the Order**: If an item is marked as unavailable in red text, do not proceed with processing the order.

  - > **Notify the Customer**: Inform the customer that the item is discontinued and is no longer available. Provide them with alternatives if available.

**1.4 Backordered Items**

→ If an item is listed as backordered with an ETA:

  - > **If ETA is less than 30 days**: Proceed with the order as usual.

  - > **If ETA is more than 30 days**: Notify the customer that the item is out of stock for a longer period and offer them the option to wait or cancel the order.

**1.5 Oversize or Heavy Items**

→ Some items may incur additional oversize charges or handling fees:

  - > These charges will appear during checkout, so always verify the total cost before completing the order.

  - > Notify the Customer: Inform the customer of any additional charges before processing the order.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER**

**2.1.1 Step 1 —** **Visit Vendor Website**

  - > Confirm the item availability by checking the item page.

  - > Ensure that the item is available for purchase.

**2.1.2 Step 2 —** **In-House Orders**

  - > If the item is in stock and the order meets the minimum order value of $1000, place the order directly via the vendor website.

  - > Only **Standard Ground** shipping option is available on vendor website.

Note - only standard ground shipping option is available on vendor website

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Check for Discontinued Items**

  - > If the item is marked as unavailable (shown in red text) on the item page, do not process the order.

  - > Immediately inform the customer that the item is discontinued and suggest alternatives if available.
