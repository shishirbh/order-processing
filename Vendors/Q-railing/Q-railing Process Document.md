# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Q-Railing orders are processed via the vendor website only.

  - > Dropship and in-house orders are both processed through the website.

  - > There is no specific day assigned for Q-railing order submissions. Orders are usually submitted on Fridays to avoid delaying customer orders.

  - > There is no free freight. All shipping costs are borne by us or the customer per policy.

  - > Standard dropship shipments use our UPS account to maintain cost control and tracking.

  - > Large freight shipments may require a Bill of Lading (BOL).

  - > Always confirm shipping method availability before processing.

  - > Always copy vendor confirmation numbers.

Always Avoid:

❌ Assuming free freight  
❌ Processing dropship if the website does not allow it  
❌ Proceeding with freight shipments without cost approval  
❌ Ignoring special handling requirements

### **1.2 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

Dropship is allowed **only if the vendor website offers a dropship shipping option**.

### **Dropship Conditions:**

✔ Shipping option available on vendor website  
✔ Shipping method confirmed before submission  
✔ Standard parcel size

### **Freight & Cost Rules:**

  - > No free freight

  - > Shipping costs must be reviewed before submission

  - > Dropship shipments use **our UPS account**

## **1.3 Handling Large Freight Items**

  - > If the item is oversized or heavy, we can provide a Bill of Lading (BOL).

  - > Provide the vendor’s location to Marina to calculate shipping costs and confirm feasibility.

  - > Shipping can only proceed once Marina approves the shipping method and cost.

## **1.4 Special Handling & Customer Responsibility**

  - > Some Q-Railing items require special handling equipment (e.g., forklift unloading).

  - > Q-Railing does not provide this service.

  - > Customers must be informed that:  
    > → They are responsible for unloading and handling.  
    > → Proper equipment may be required at delivery.

  - > Failure to communicate may result in delivery refusal or safety risks.

## **1.5 Low Stock Approval Protocol**

If a vendor order contains **low quantity availability**:

1.  > Inform Elina.

2.  > She will either:
    
      - > approve increasing stock levels, or
    
      - > approve shipping charges for in-house processing.

This ensures inventory continuity and avoids order delays.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**Eligibility:  
**Used when items are not suitable for dropship or when approved for in-house fulfillment.

### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Add all required items via the vendor website.

### **2.1.2 Step 2 — Shipping Address**

  - > Select the **DK warehouse address**.

### **2.1.3 Step 3 — Shipping Method**

  - > Select standard shipping and review costs.

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Low Stock Check**

If any line shows low availability:

  - > Inform Elina before proceeding.

### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Final Review**

Verify:

  - > items & quantities

  - > shipping cost

  - > warehouse address

### **2.1.6 Step 6 — Submit Order**

  - > Place order and record confirmation number.

**2.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Confirm Dropship Availability**

  - > Verify dropship option is available on the vendor website.

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Ensure correct items and specifications are selected.

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Enter Customer Address**

  - > Enter shipping details accurately.

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Shipping Method**

  - > Select shipping option using **our UPS account**.

### **2.2.5 Step 5 — Review Shipping Cost**

  - > Confirm cost viability before proceeding.

### **2.2.6 Step 6 — Final Review & Submit**

  - > Verify items, address, and shipping method.

  - > Place order and record confirmation number.

**2.3 PROCESSING DROPSHIP ORDERS — LARGE / FREIGHT ITEMS**

### **2.3.1 Step 1 — Identify Freight Item**

  - > Confirm if item is oversized or heavy.

### **2.3.2 Step 2 — Obtain Vendor Location**

  - > Provide vendor location to Marina for cost calculation.

### **2.3.3 Step 3 — Request Shipping Cost Evaluation**

  - > Marina confirms feasibility and cost.

### **2.3.4 Step 4 — Decision**

✔ If approved → proceed  
❌ If not approved → inform customer

### **2.3.5 Step 5 — Provide Bill of Lading (if required)**

  - > Supply BOL when arranging freight.

### **2.3.6 Step 6 — Submit Order**

  - > Proceed only after approval.

  - > Place order and record confirmation number.
