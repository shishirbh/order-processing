# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Haven Technology vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

  - > Fulfillment Model is Dropship ONLY via UPS

  - > Haven uses standard shipping for all orders

  - > There are no minimum order value requirements (MOV)

  - > No freight approvals required

  - > No Marina involvement

  - > Do NOT request special shipping unless explicitly approved by leadership

### **1.2 Submission Cycle**

  - > Haven orders are processed as they are received

  - > No weekly cut-off

  - > No batching

  - > Orders should be submitted immediately once classified and verified

**Note:** Delays increase build queue risk for configured equipment.

**1.3 Mandatory Order Classification (CRITICAL STEP)**

Every Haven order MUST be classified before submission.

There are **ONLY two valid order types**:

### **1.3.1 Type 1: Normal Dropship Orders (No Addons):**

Applies when **ALL** conditions are met:

  - > Customer purchases a standard communication unit

  - > NO addons

  - > NO configuration

  - > NO customization

📌 These are processed as **standard dropship orders**.

### **1.3.2 Type 2: Addon / Configured Equipment Orders (MANDATORY DROPSHIP):**

Applies when **ANY** condition is met:

  - > Customer selects **one or more addons**

  - > Equipment is **customized or configured**

  - > Unit is **assembled per customer selection**

**Example:**

  - > SC-100LBR-2 with addons (Figure 1)

📌 **ALL configured orders MUST be dropshipped.  
**📌 Haven assembles these units per customer—accuracy is critical.

**1.4 Addon Processing Rules**

If an order includes **addons**, you MUST complete **BOTH steps below**:

**1.4.1 Enter Addons in Order Inline Comments (MANDATORY)**

  - > Add **ALL addon details** in **Order Inline Comments (**as seen in Figure 1)

  - > Follow the **exact format** used in the standard Purchasing reference image

  - > Include:
    
      - > Base model
    
      - > Full addon list
    
      - > Clear, readable structure

> ![](media/image3.png)  
> *Figure 2: Addon details in Order Inline Comments*

**Why this matters:**

  - > Creates audit trail

  - > Protects against disputes

  - > Allows Customer Service to verify configuration

⚠️ **Never rely only on the email. Inline comments are mandatory.**

### **1.4.2 Include Addons in Vendor Email (MANDATORY)**

Your email to Haven **MUST explicitly list**:

  - > Base model (e.g., SC-100LBR-2)

  - > Every addon selected

**Example with image:**

SC-100LBR-2 with:

\- Addon A  
\- Addon B  
\- Addon C

![](media/image1.png)  
*Figure 3: Selection of Addons*

🚨 If addons are unclear or missing:

  - > Wrong unit may be built

  - > Delays, returns, and customer escalation will occur

## **1.5 Packing & Invoice Control** 

For every Haven dropship order:

  - > Invoice must NOT be included

  - > Packing slip must NOT be included

## **1.6 Mandatory Follow-Up (DO NOT SKIP)**

Every Haven order **MUST receive BOTH**:

  - > Order Acknowledgment (OA)

  - > Tracking Number

📌 **Do NOT close the order** until **both are received and verified**.

→ For **1 Business Day or 2 Business Day orders**, Haven ships using **our UPS account**.

Please ship directly to our customer.

SHIP CUSTOMER NEXT DAY COLLECT USING OUR UPS ACCOUNT \#A21W92

Kindly let us know if any changes.

Regards,

Purchasing Team

This ensures:

  - > Shipping is billed to **our UPS account**

  - > Vendor ships immediately
