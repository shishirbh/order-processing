# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Jeske Company Inc. vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > Customer orders are dropshipped if they meet the minimum order value depending on the item as discussed in Section 1.2.

  - > Standard ETA for In-stock items → 7 calendar days

  - > Backorders are Allowed:

<!-- end list -->

  - > Jeske website always shows stock availability

  - > No manual confirmation required

<!-- end list -->

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) Rules:

<!-- end list -->

  - > Standard Items → $350

  - > Oversized / Freight Items (≥48” OR ≥25 lbs) → $1,500

**⚠️ Important Clarification**

  - > Any item 48 inches or longer OR weighing 25 lbs or more is treated as Oversized, even if it ships via FedEx/UPS.

  - > Oversized non-freight items follow the SAME MOV rules as freight.

# **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Dropship below MOV  
❌ Sell Allegion-brand items  
❌ Forget “PLEASE BLIND SHIP” (Vendor cost is hidden in a ‘blind ship’)  
❌ Place expedited order without customer approval on extra charges  
❌ Override warehouse shipping address

### **1.2 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

**Dropship is allowed ONLY if all conditions below are met:**

  - > Item is in stock on Jeske website

  - > Correct MOV threshold is met

  - > Item is not restricted

#### **Eligibility Summary:**

  - > Standard items: Dropship allowed at $350+

  - > Oversized / Freight items: Dropship allowed at $1,500+

  - > MOV not met:
    
      - > Jeske will charge shipping, OR
    
      - > Processor must inform customer, OR
    
      - > Move to in-house vendor order if total in-house order reaches $1,500

**1.3 Restricted & Discontinued Items**

### **1.3.1 Restricted Items — DO NOT SELL**

Never sell Allegion brand items, including:

  - > Falcon

  - > Ives

  - > LCN

  - > Schlage

  - > Stanley

  - > Von Duplin

  - > Safelock

  - > Baldwin

  - > Kwikset

### **1.3.2 If restricted item encountered:**

Check alternate vendors:

  - > SecLock

  - > Top Notch

  - > IML Security Supply (IMLSS)

  - > Banner Solutions

### **1.3.3 Discontinued Items:**

Vendor Website shows “Item Not Found”. Action Required:

  - > Inform customer immediately

  - > Offer alternatives (if available)

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 STANDARD GROUND DROPSHIP**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 —** Add items to cart

### **2.1.2 Step 2 —** Confirm MOV met ($350 for small items standard ground dropship)

### **2.1.3 Step 3 —** Select $0 shipping (FedEx/UPS)

### **2.1.4 Step 4 —** Paste customer address in order notes

### **2.1.5 Step 5 —** Add “PLEASE BLIND SHIP”

# **2.2 EXPEDITED DROPSHIP (1-2 DAYS)**

**2.2.1 Step 1 —** Follow standard dropship steps

**2.2.2 Step 2 —** Select cheaper FedEx/UPS expedited option

**2.2.3 Step 3 —** Compare shipping cost vs customer-paid amount

**2.2.4 Step 4 —** Inform customer if extra charges apply**  
**

**2.3 FREIGHT DROPSHIP**

**2.3.1 Step 1 —** Allowed only if MOV ≥ $1,500

**2.3.2 Step 2 —** Select Freight $0 shipping

**2.3.3 Step 3 —** Add customer address in notes with “PLEASE BLIND SHIP”

# **2.4 NON-FREIGHT OVERSIZED ITEM**

**2.4.1 Step 1 — Same steps and rules as freight items**

**2.4.2 Step 2 — If MOV met ($1,500):**

  - > Select $0 shipping

**OR**

**2.4.2 Step 2 — If MOV NOT met:**

  - > Jeske charges shipping

  - > Options:
    
      - > Inform customer of added cost
    
      - > Move to in-house vendor order if total reaches $1,500
