# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Dot Food vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > All customer orders are processed dropshipped unless they qualify for international handling.

  - > Shipping costs can be verified on vendor website.

  - > Vendor typically ships next business day.

  - > Dot Food does NOT support backorders.

  - > Stock can change rapidly. Always re-check correct item number, case quantity and stock right before checkout

  - > Orders are processed only if items show in stock.

  - > If an item shows “Special Order” on the website, it must not be ordered.

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Ordering out-of-stock items  
❌ Direct international dropshipping  
❌ Skipping customer phone/email  
❌ Guessing shipping methods

### **1.2 Order Types & Eligibility**

**1.2.1 Standard Customer Orders**

  - > Default processing method: Dropship

  - > Allowed only for domestic U.S. orders

  - > Item must be in stock at time of ordering

**1.2.2 Expedited Orders (1–2 Business Day)**

  - > Allowed only if:
    
      - > Item is in stock
    
      - > Order is domestic U.S.
    
      - > Customer phone number + email are entered

  - > Expedited shipping is selected on the vendor website

**1.2.3 International Orders (Critical Rule)**

  - > Direct international dropshipping is NOT allowed. Always add to or create In-House order.

  - > All international orders must follow this path:
    
      - > Vendor → Our U.S. Warehouse (Florida) → Customer

<!-- end list -->

  - > Ensure sufficient shipping coverage, as shipping is paid twice (to us, then to the customer).

<!-- end list -->

  - > Processor must:
    
      - > STOP
    
      - > Contact In-House International Team before ordering

⚠️ **International orders are not expedited** — inform customers that they ship **ground only**.

⚠️ **Never place an international order without in-house approval**

**1.3 Stock & Backorder Rules**

  - > Dot Food does not allow backorders

  - > If item shows:
    
      - > Out of stock
    
      - > Limited availability
    
      - > Delayed availability

⚠️ **DO NOT PLACE THE ORDER**

Required Action:

  - > Pause processing

  - > Notify supervisor / CX team

**1.4 Shipping Methods & Rules**

| **Shipping Type** | **How to Process**               | **Mandatory Notes**        |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Standard Ground   | Website                          | Domestic only              |

⚠️**Important Clarification:**

  - > These must ship to U.S. warehouse first

**1.5 Mandatory Customer Information**

Before placing any order, confirm:

  - > Full Customer Name

  - > Correct Shipping Address
    
      - > U.S. address only for international orders

  - > Valid Phone Number

  - > Valid Email Address

❌ Missing any of the above → **DO NOT PLACE ORDER**

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Log into vendor website**

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Search and select exact SKU

  - > Add items one by one

  - > Match quantity exactly with customer order

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Edit PO Details**

  - > Enter internal PO format

  - > PO must match DKH order number

  - > No shortcuts or placeholders

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Checkout**

  - > Select correct shipping method:
    
      - > Standard Ground
    
      - > 1–2 Business Day (if applicable)

**2.1.5 Step 5 — Enter Customer Details**

  - > Enter all mandatory customer information

  - > For international:
    
      - > Use our U.S. warehouse address only

**2.1.6 Step 6 — Final Review (Mandatory Pause Point)**

  - > Verify:
    
      - > SKUs
    
      - > Quantities
    
      - > Shipping address
    
      - > Shipping method

**2.1.7 Step 7 — Place order only after full verification**
