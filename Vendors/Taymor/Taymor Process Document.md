# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 MANDATORY FIRST STEP - Orders Classification**

Before taking any action, classify the order across four dimensions:

  - > **Destination:**
    
      - > US (Mainland)
    
      - > US Islands (HI, AK, PR)
    
      - > International

  - > **Tax Status:**
    
      - > Tax-Exempt State
    
      - > Non-Tax-Exempt State

  - > **Shipping Type:**
    
      - > Standard Parcel
    
      - > Freight (Oversized / Palletized)

  - > **Order Value:**
    
      - > ≥ $750
    
      - > \< $750

**No processing may begin until classification is complete.**

### **1.2 Vendor Rules**

  - > Taymor vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

  - > Every order must be processed by Friday

  - > Orders cannot be held past Friday for consolidation

  - > Dropship is allowed ONLY where explicitly permitted (Section 1.4)

  - > Freight orders require verification before submission

  - > FedEx and BOL instructions must NEVER be mixed

  - > Monitor inbox after order submission for:
    
      - > Confirmations
    
      - > Reverts
    
      - > Stock updates
    
      - > Dimension requests

  - > Respond promptly to avoid delays

## **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Holding orders past Friday  
❌ Dropshipping non-tax-exempt states  
❌ Dropshipping international freight  
❌ Mixing FedEx + BOL instructions  
❌ Skipping freight verification

### **1.3 IN-HOUSE ORDERS**

**1.3.1 When In-House Processing is Required:**

Process as **In-House** if **any** of the following apply:

  - > Order is International

  - > Order ships to a Non-Tax-Exempt State

  - > Order is not approved for dropship

  - > Order is shipping to HI, AK, or PR

  - > Order is International Freight

  - > Small Items

**1.3.2 $750 Target Minimum Rule:**

  - > Target minimum for in-house orders: $750

  - > Purpose: Shipping efficiency and cost optimization

⚠️ This is a target, not a blocker.

**1.3.3 Friday Exception Rule (ABSOLUTE):**

If vendor order value does NOT reach $750 by Friday:

  - > Order MUST still be processed

  - > Ship using our FedEx account \#632647611

  - > Do NOT delay beyond Friday

  - > No carryover allowed. Friday deadline overrides consolidation goals.

❌ Delaying past Friday is a compliance violation.

### **1.3.4 FedEx Rule for In-House Orders:**

  - > Use general email draft

  - > Do NOT mention FedEx account in the email

  - > FedEx account is applied operationally, not communicated

### **1.4 DROPSHIP ORDERS (STANDARD – NON-FREIGHT)**

### **1.4.1 Eligibility Criteria (ALL must be true):**

An order qualifies for Standard Dropship only if:

  - > Shipping destination is within the United States

  - > Shipping destination is a Tax-Exempt State

  - > Order is NOT freight

  - > Order is NOT international

  - > Order is NOT shipping to HI, AK, or PR

  - > Is not a small item

If any condition fails → Dropship is NOT allowed.

### **1.4.2 FedEx Rule (Standard Dropship):**

  - > FedEx account number is mandatory

  - > Must be explicitly mentioned in the dropship email

  - > Applies to all standard dropship orders

→ Never omit the FedEx account on dropships.

### **1.4.3 FedEx Rule for Dropship Orders:**

  - > Dropship email must include our FedEx account number

  - > Orders must not be sent without FedEx account reference

**Always Verify:  
**✔ Customer address accuracy  
✔ Tax-exempt status  
✔ Correct email template usage

**1.5 FREIGHT ORDER PROTOCOL**

### **1.5.1 Freight Classification:**

An order is Freight if any of the following apply:

  - > Item is oversized

  - > Item is palletized

  - > Weight or dimensions exceed standard parcel limits

When in doubt → treat as freight and verify.

### **1.5.2 Mandatory Shipping Verification:**

No freight order may proceed without confirmation.

  - > **Domestic (US) Freight:  
    > ** → Verify shipping with **Marina  
    > **

  - > **International Freight:  
    > ** → Verify shipping with **Justin**

→ Unverified freight = processing violation

### **1.5.4 Absolute Freight Rule:**

**Never mix:**

  - > FedEx account instructions  
    > **AND**

  - > BOL / Freight instructions

→ Doing so causes shipment failures.
