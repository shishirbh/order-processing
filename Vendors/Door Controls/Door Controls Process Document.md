# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Minimum Order Value: $1,500

  - > Order Processing Method: All orders processed through the DCU vendor website

  - > Standard ETA: 7 days for in-stock items

  - > Backorders: Allowed except for discontinued items

### **Always Avoid:**

> ❌ Selecting the wrong shipping addresses
> 
> ❌ Ignoring discount or free shipping thresholds
> 
> ❌ Dropping orders with missing phone numbers on expedited shipments
> 
> ❌ Submitting discontinued items

### **Example Scenarios:**

1.  > $1,600 order with no core items:**  
    > ** → Process in-house; apply 2% discount; check free shipping.

2.  > $1,800 expedited with core items:  
    > → Escalate to CS(need core fee approval), confirm with Manas/Prathamesh, add phone, dropship.

3.  > $1,200 order with 9-ft item:  
    > → Escalate to Elina(need to check freight); decide case-by-case if dropship allowed.

### **1.2 Core Items**

  - > This vendor supplies a special category of products known as “core items.” Core items require an upfront *core fee*, which functions as a refundable deposit.

  - > The core fee is refunded to the customer in case they return the core item to DK Hardware.

A product should be treated as a core item when all the following indicators are present:

1.  > Backoffice Manuf \# Format:
    
      - > The Manufacturer Number (Manuf\#) in Backoffice always begins with the letter “R” for core items (See Figure 1).

2.  > Core Fee Line Item:
    
      - > A separate line item appears in the order, and the Manuf \# column explicitly shows “CoreFee” (See Figure 1).**  
        > **

3.  > Vendor Website Notice:
    
      - > The item listing on the vendor’s website clearly includes a message such as:  
        > “Core is due in 90 days” (See Figure 2).

### **1.3 Shipping & Dropship Criteria**

### **When Dropship is Allowed:**

  - > Order value ≥ $1,500

  - > Customer expedited shipping (1–2 day delivery)

  - > 8+ ft long items (after escalation as mentioned in point 1.3)  
    > → Ex-DKH-2826069,DKH-2817033

#### **1.4 When to Escalate**

**Escalate to Elina (MFG) if:**

  - > Item length \> 8 ft

**Escalate to Manas/Prathamesh (Shipping) for:**

  - > Expedited shipping confirmation

  - > Customer phone or contact is missing

### **1.5 Special Requirements**

  - > Always verify free-shipping eligibility (≥ $1,500).

  - > Apply 2% discount before submission.

  - > Add note in vendor order: “USE our FedEx account” when applicable.

  - > Address Selection: Always use DK Hardware as the shipping address.

  - > Never select unapproved address options.

  - > Internal Communication:
    
      - > Keep notes clear and consistent for audits.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

### **2.1 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Cross-Check Items**

  - > Confirm all customer-ordered items.

  - > Verify all items belong to DCU vendor flow.

### **2.1.2 Step 2 — Pricing Verification**

  - > Match vendor website prices.

  - > Verify if order qualifies for free shipping or dropship.

### **2.1.3 Step 3 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Add confirmed items only.

  - > Ensure cart total = vendor order total.

  - > Record cart total in vendor order notes.

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Stock Check**

  - > Confirm in-stock or backorder eligibility.

  - > If discontinued → Do not proceed.

  - > Note backorders clearly.

### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Checkout**

  - > Select address: DK Hardware (when applicable).

  - > Follow website prompts carefully.

  - > Use *Enter Contact* if required

### **2.1.6 Step 6 — Payment  
**

  - > Enter **PO number**.

### **2.1.7 Step 7 — Review**

  - > Double-check items, address, totals, and notes.

  - > Attach a screenshot if required.

### **2.1.8 Step 8 — Discount**

  - > Apply **2% discount** as “MISC” line item.

### **2.1.9 Step 9 — Finalize**

  - > Finalize the order.

  - > Record the order confirmation number in the Vendor Order

## **2.2 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — DROPSHIP**

**2.2.1 Step 1 — Review Incoming Order**

  - > Identify order type:
    
      - > Expedited
    
      - > Standard
    
      - > Vendor order

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Order value
    
      - > Threshold rules
    
      - > Core item involvement

**2.2.2 Step 2 — Assign Routing**

  - > Based on stock + dropship eligibility

**2.2.3 Step 3 —** **Enter Shipping Info**

  - > Use complete customer details

  - > Include phone number

**2.2.4 Step 4 —** **Add PO Details**

  - > Ensure PO matches internal record

**2.2.5 Step 5 —** **Final Checks**

  - > Validate dropship approval (Elina)

  - > Confirm order status (New → Dropship).

**2.2.6 Step 6 —** **Place Order**

  - > Submit order on vendor portal

  - > Record confirmation number/vendor order ID

  - > Update internal system with:
    
      - > Shipment method
    
      - > Dropship status
    
      - > Follow-up requirements
