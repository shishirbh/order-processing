# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > WPG customer orders are processed through email via Backoffice only.

  - > Most WPG orders are processed as dropship.

  - > Only very small, easy-to-ship items may be moved in-house.

  - > There is no minimum order value — fulfillment decisions are based solely on size, handling, and shipping practicality.

## **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Moving bulky, heavy, or freight items through the warehouse unnecessarily  
❌ Dropshipping small items that are easier and safer to ship in-house  
❌ Processing large items without confirming shipping dimensions  
❌ Releasing freight shipments before Marina approval  
❌ Allowing freight shipments to move without issuing a BOL  
❌ Assuming parcel eligibility for large or heavy items  
❌ Skirting dimension verification for unclear items  
❌ Ignoring shipping cost approval requirements

### **  
1.2 Fulfillment Decision Standard**

  - > Small, compact, easy items → In-House

  - > Bulky, heavy, long, fragile, or awkward items → Dropship

### **1.3 Dropship Rules**

WPG orders are normally dropshipped directly to the customer.

**Use Dropship When:**

  - > Item is not small

  - > Item is bulky, long, heavy, fragile, or awkward

  - > Shipping directly to the customer is more efficient than routing through our warehouse

### **Before Sending Large or Heavy Items:**

  - > Confirm shipping dimensions with the vendor if not available.

  - > Share dimensions with **Marina** for freight or shipping cost approval.

**1.4 Freight Handling Procedures**

Some WPG items may ship via freight instead of parcel.

**Freight Indicators:**

If the item is:

  - > Large

  - > Long

  - > Heavy

  - > Crated or palletized

**Required Actions:**

  - > Obtain shipping dimensions from the vendor.

  - > Submit dimensions to **Marina** for shipping cost approval.

  - > Once approved, the order may be dropshipped.

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **→** Used only for small, compact, easy-to-ship items.

**STEPS:**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Verify & Move Order to Submit**

### Before submitting, confirm:

  - ### Pricing correct

  - ### Quantities accurate

  - ### Item qualifies as small and easy to ship

  - ### Shipping via warehouse is practical

  - ### No freight risk or special handling required

  - > No restricted notes or special shipping requirements

### Then → Move vendor order to SUBMIT.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Process Through Email**

  - ### In Backoffice:

  - ### Click By Email

  - ### Select correct shipping template

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Insert Correct Draft Template**

### Use:

  - ### **Standard In-House Template  
    ** → For warehouse fulfillment of small items

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Final Pre-Send Check**

### Before hitting send, confirm:

### ✔ Correct subject line  
✔ Correct email draft inserted  
✔ No internal notes remain  
✔ Item qualifies for in-house handling

# **3.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

→ Default method for most WPG items

**STEPS:**

**3.2.1 Step 1 — Create the vendor order in the BO**

**3.2.2 Step 2 — Verify the customer shipping address (mandatory)**

**3.2.3 Step 3 — Confirm Item Eligibility**

Use dropship when:

  - > Item is not small

  - > Item is bulky, long, heavy, fragile, or awkward

  - > Direct shipment is more efficient than routing through warehouse

**3.2.4 Step 4 — Confirm Dimensions if Needed**

If dimensions are not available:

  - > Request shipping dimensions from vendor.

  - > Share dimensions with **Marina** if shipping cost or feasibility is unclear.

### **3.2.5 Step 5 — Submit Dropship Request**

Send request via:

  - > DK Hardware Back Office email

  - > Standard Dropship Email Template

### **3.2.6 Step 6 — Include Shipping Account** 

If shipment is sent using our account:

→ Include FedEx Account \#632647611

# **3.3 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — FREIGHT DROPSHIP**

**3.3.1 Step 1 — Request shipping dimensions**

Obtain from vendor:

  - > Dimensions

  - > Weight

  - > Packaging details (crate / pallet if applicable)

**3.3.2 Step 2 — Submit to Marina for Approval**

Share dimensions with Marina to confirm:

  - > Shipping feasibility

  - > Freight cost approval

**3.3.3 Step 3 — Confirm Shipping Method**

If freight is required:

  - > Wait for approval before proceeding.

**3.3.4 Step 4 — Once approved:**

  - > Proceed with dropship

  - > Use Bill of Lading (BOL)

**3.3.5 Step 5 — Submit Freight Dropship Request**

Send using:  
→ Freight Dropship Email Template

**Do not release shipment until:**

✔ Vendor confirms order is ready  
✔ BOL has been issued and provided

**SECTION 4 — DECISION MATRIX**

Table 1 - WPG Processing Decision Matrix

| **Scenario**               | **Item Characteristics**     | **Required Action**          | **Final Decision**        | **Notes**                    |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Small, compact item        | Light, easy to pack          | Process through warehouse    | In-House                  | Preferred for easy handling  |
| Small but fragile item     | Damage risk during handling  | Evaluate handling risk       | Dropship if safer         | Prevent breakage             |
| Standard item              | Not small                    | Ship directly to customer    | Dropship                  | Default method               |
| Bulky or awkward item      | Large or difficult to handle | Confirm dimensions & cost    | Dropship                  | Avoid double handling        |
| Long item                  | Extended length              | Verify dimensions            | Dropship                  | Warehouse handling not ideal |
| Heavy item                 | High weight                  | Send dimensions to Marina    | Dropship (after approval) | Cost review required         |
| Freight-required item      | Crated / palletized          | Obtain dimensions & approval | Dropship (after approval) | BOL may be required          |
| Freight shipment ready     | Freight approved             | Issue BOL before release     | Proceed                   | Required control step        |
| Dimensions unavailable     | Large or unclear item        | Request dimensions           | Hold                      | Do not proceed               |
| Shipping cost not approved | Freight/high cost            | Await Marina approval        | Hold                      | Prevent cost issues          |
