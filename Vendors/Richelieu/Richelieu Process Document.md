# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Richelieu vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements is $650 → Free Shipping for orders over $650.

  - > Shipping method is determined by Richelieu and not by us.

  - > Shipping method and charges are determined solely by Richelieu.

  - > Verify ETA and shipping at final review page

  - > Escalate laminate items immediately (Section 1.5.2).

> *→ Laminate items* - we do not sell Laminate items. These are very fragile rolled in a large roll but usually crack and breaks apart and hence, we will not fulfill this order
> 
> → Laminate items - we do not sell Laminate items.
> 
> those should be removed from our website

  - > Use warehouse address for all in-house orders

## **Always Avoid:**

❌ Never assume stock availability  
❌ Never dropship laminate items  
❌ Never promise expedited shipping without ETA confirmation  
❌ Never override warehouse-only shipping flags

### **1.2 Stock & ETA Visibility**

  - > Richelieu does NOT display stock availability.

  - > ETA and shipping charges are only visible on the final review page before placing the order.

  - > Processors must never assume stock or ETA prior to checkout.

### **1.3 In-House Order Rules** 

Process the order IN-HOUSE when:

  - > Customer has not paid sufficient shipping

  - > Item is marked “This item only ships to our warehouse address”

  - > Item is Laminate

### **1.4 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

### **Allowed:**

  - > Standard Ground Dropshipping is allowed even if MOV ($650) is NOT met, only if the customer has paid sufficient shipping to cover vendor charges.

### **Not Allowed:**

  - > Dropshipping is **NOT allowed** if:
    
      - > Customer has not paid enough shipping
    
      - > Item is flagged “Ships only to warehouse”
    
      - > Item is Laminate (see Section 1.5.2)

## **1.5 Special Handling & Restricted Items**

### **1.5.1 Warehouse-Only Items**

  - > Items flagged “This item only ships to our warehouse address”:
    
      - > Must ship to DK Hardware warehouse
    
      - > Must be forwarded from warehouse to customer
    
      - > Customer must be informed of:
        
          - > Vendor → Warehouse shipping
        
          - > Warehouse → Customer shipping

### **1.5.2 Laminate Items – HARD STOP**

  - > Laminate items are NOT sold or fulfilled

  - > Reason:
    
      - > Extremely fragile
    
      - > Shipped in large rolls
    
      - > High breakage/cracking risk

  - > Action Required
    
      - > DO NOT process order
    
      - > Escalate to Elina for confirmation
    
      - > Cancel or block fulfillment

## **1.6 Customer Communication Rules**

### **Must Notify Customer:**

  - > Additional shipping or packing fees

  - > Warehouse forwarding charges

  - > ETA greater than 1 month

  - > Expedited shipping downgrade

### **Do NOT Notify Customer:**

  - > Backordered items **with no ETA** (process silently)

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**STEPS:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Go to Cart on the vendor website**

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Add all required items to the cart**

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Click Proceed to Checkout**

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Under Shipping Address:**

  - > Select the saved warehouse address.

**2.1.5 Step 5 — On the Final Review page:**

  - > Review ETA for each item.

  - > Confirm shipping charges (free over $650).

  - > If ETA is over 1 month, inform the customer.

  - > If no ETA, process as backordered (no customer notice required).

**2.1.6 Step 6 —** **Enter**

  - > PO Number

  - > Manual ETA for each item (based on review page)

**2.1.7 Step 7 —** **Double-check:**

  - > Items, quantities, address, ETA, and charges

**2.1.8 Step 8 —** **Place the order**

**2.1.9 Step 9 —** **Download order confirmation and paste it on the order.**

**2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - DROPSHIP (STANDARD GROUND AND EXPEDITED)**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Go to Cart on the vendor website**

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Add dropship items to the cart**

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Click Proceed to Checkout**

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Under Shipping Address:**

  - > Use the DKHARDWARE KEY extension to auto-fill the password.

  - > Manually enter the customer’s shipping address.

### **2.2.5 Step 5 — On the Final Review page:**

  - > Review ETA and shipping charges.

  - > Inform the customer of any additional shipping or packing fees.

  - > If ETA is over 1 month, inform the customer.

  - > If no ETA, process as backordered.

### **2.2.6 Step 6 — Enter:**

  - > PO Number

  - > Manual ETA for each item

### **2.2.7 Step 7 — Expedited Dropship Rules**

  - > If ETA is 1–2 business days → process as Expedited Dropship.

  - > If ETA is more than 2 business days → inform the customer and ship via standard ground.

### **2.2.8 Step 8 — Double-check all details**

### **2.2.9 Step 9 — Place the order.**

### **2.2.10 Step 10 — Copy the confirmation number and log it in the internal vendor system**
