**1.3 Dropship Restrictions (Hard Stops)**

Dropship is **NOT allowed** if any of the following apply:

  - > Zoro orders (no exceptions) - Zoro pickup occurs at the DK Hardware warehouse.

  - > Marketplace orders:
    
      - > Amazon
    
      - > Walmart
    
      - > eBay
    
      - > Wayfair (Dropship only if freight + free shipping)

  - > Alaska (AK)

  - > Hawaii (HI)

  - > Puerto Rico (PR)

  - > Canada

  - > International addresses

  - > Islands

  - > Military addresses

  - > PO Boxes

  - > Pickup orders

  - > Customer provides own shipping account

⚠️ **If any condition applies → Process In-House.**

### **1.4 Dropship Eligibility**

Dropship is allowed if no restrictions apply and any of the following conditions are met:

### **1.4.1 Ships from Manufacturer:**

  - > If item shows “Ships from Manufacturer” on the portal.

### **1.4.2 Oversized / Heavy Items:**

**Dropship recommended if:**

  - > Weight \> 5 lbs

  - > Length ≥ 18 inches

**Examples include:**

  - > gas vents

  - > medicine cabinets

  - > long extrusions

### **1.4.3 Fast ETA:**

Dropship allowed if portal shows:

  - > Ships Next Day

  - > 2–3 Days Delivery

**1.4.4 Value-Based Eligibility:**

Table 1 - Shipping Costs

| **Order Value** | **Shipping**                  |
| --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| ≥ $150          | Free Shipping                 |
| \< $150         | $12.99 Onwards shipping costs |

⚠️ **Note:**

  - > Portal may show $0 shipping but invoice may later include shipping charges.

  - > HD Supply may charge $10 handling fee for orders under $50.

### **1.5 In-House Order Rules**

→ In-House orders should only be processed when:

  - > Vendor Order total **≥ $150**.

→ If below:

  - > hold order

  - > combine with stock items.

→ Oversized items:

  - > If item **\>5 lbs or ≥18 inches**

  - > → Convert to **Dropship instead**.

  - > both icons turn green.

**2.4.5 Step 5 — Open Vendor Order**

**2.4.7 Step 7 — Search and verify item**

**2.4.9 Step 9 — Enter DKH vendor PO \#**

**2.4.10 Step 10 — Go to “Enter Alternate Address”**

**2.4.11 Step 11 — Add customer name + phone in NAME field**

  - > Example:  
    > → John Smith (832-xxx-4900)

  - > Confirm ETA(If it shows backordered- we cant dropship)

**SECTION 3 — DECISION MATRIX**

Table 2 - US Lock Processing Decision Matrix

| **Scenario**                                                | **Action**                   | **Notes**                                                     |
| ----------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Item eligible for dropship                                  | Dropship                     | Ensure no marketplace or address restrictions apply.          |
| Weight \> 5 lbs or length ≥ 18″                             | Prefer Dropship              | Common for long hardware items (vents, cabinets, extrusions). |
| ETA shows Next Day / 2–3 Days                               | Dropship allowed             | ETA priority overrides order value rules.                     |
| Order ≥ $150                                                | Dropship                     | Shipping typically free.                                      |
| Order \< $150                                               | Compare cost                 | US Lock shipping \~$12.99; check HD Supply total cost.        |
| Ships from Manufacturer                                     | Compare US Lock vs HD Supply | Choose vendor with lower total cost including shipping.       |
| Item Backordered                                            | Do not dropship              | Check alternate vendors or process in-house.                  |
| Partial stock available                                     | Process In-House             | Avoid splitting dropship shipments.                           |
| While Supplies Last                                         | Treat as discontinued        | Suggest replacement item.                                     |
| Marketplace orders (Amazon/Walmart/eBay)                    | In-House only                | Dropship not permitted.                                       |
| Wayfair orders                                              | Dropship only if freight     | Only when freight with free shipping.                         |
| Zoro orders                                                 | In-House only                | Zoro picks up from DKH warehouse.                             |
| Restricted address (AK/HI/PR/Canada/Intl, PO Box, Military) | In-House only                | Dropship not allowed to these locations.                      |
