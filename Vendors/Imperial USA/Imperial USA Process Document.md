# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Imperial USA orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements are discussed in Section 1.2.

  - > Shipping methods depend on Item Type as discussed in Section 1.3.

  - > Stock availability is visible on item page on vendor website.

  - > Dropship is allowed for expedited orders only (strict conditions)

### **1.2 Minimum Order Value (MOV)**

Table 1

| **Scenario**    | **Rule**                                |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Any order       | $100 minimum required to place order    |
| Small items     | Free shipping at $1000+                 |
| Oversized items | Shipping charges apply even above $1000 |

**Example:**

  - > $1,200 small hardware → free shipping

  - > $1,200 oversized frame → shipping still applies (check Section 1.3)

⚠️ **Always Avoid:**

❌ Assuming $1000+ = free shipping for oversized items

### **1.3 Shipping Rules (By Item Type)** 

### **1.3.1 Small Items**

  - > Free shipping only if order ≥ $1000

  - > Below $1000 → standard vendor shipping applies

### **1.3.2 Oversized (Non-Freight) Items**

  - > FedEx account is mandatory

  - > FedEx account must be entered in order notes

**Required Note Format:**

> Customer FedEx Account: XXXXXXX

⚠️ **Always Avoid:**

❌ Placing oversized orders without a FedEx account  
❌ Assuming vendor absorbs oversized shipping

### **1.3.3 Freight Items**

  - > DO NOT auto-ship

  - > BOL must be provided later

**Mandatory Note (enter 3 times):**

> *“WHEN ORDER IS READY TO SHIP, PLEASE LET US KNOW AND WE WILL PROVIDE BOL”*

⚠️ **Always Avoid:**

❌ Allowing vendor to ship freight without BOL coordination

### **1.4 Doors & Door Frames (CRITICAL CONTROL)**

### **1.4.1 Mandatory Customer Quote**

  - > Signed customer quote is REQUIRED

  - > Applies to all doors & door frames

### **1.4.2 Out-of-Stock Doors**

  - > Vendor quote required before processin**g  
    > **

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Price
    
      - > Availability
    
      - > ETA

### **1.4.3 Quote Verification**

Before ordering, verify:

  - > Price matches quote

  - > Quantity matches quote

  - > SKU/item matches quote

  - > ETA should also be included if applicable

⚠️ **Always Avoid:**

❌ Processing doors without a signed customer quote  
❌ Assuming quoted pricing without verification

## **1.5 Stock Visibility & ETA Handling**

Table 2 - Stock Status Rules

| **Status**     | **Action**                               |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| In Stock       | Process normally                         |
| ETA ≤ 1 month  | Process order                            |
| ETA \> 1 month | Inform customer before ordering          |
| No ETA         | Treat as backorder → confirm with vendor |

**Example:**

  - > ETA = 3 weeks → OK to process

  - > ETA = 6 weeks → customer must be informed first

## **1.6 Expedited Orders & Dropshipping Rules**

### **1.6.1 Dropship Eligibility**

Dropshipping is allowed ONLY IF ALL conditions below are met:

1.  > Order is Expedited

2.  > Item is In Stock

3.  > Customer address entered

4.  > FedEx account entered

🚫 **Standard ground orders are usually not dropshipped to save shipping charges**

**1.6.2 Expedited Order Value Rule**

Table 3

| **Order Value** | **Action**                                                     |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ≥ $100          | ✔ Eligible for expedited shipping                              |
| \< $100         | Ground shipping only (ordered in-house) — must inform customer |

**Example:**

  - > $85 expedited request → convert to ground + notify customer

## **1.7 Backorder Rules**

  - > Always confirm vendor ETA**  
    > **

  - > If ETA is:
    
      - > Long
    
      - > Unknown
    
      - > Missing  
        > → Customer must be informed before proceeding

⚠️ **Always Avoid:**

❌ Placing backorders without ETA confirmation  
❌ Assuming ETA from previous orders

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**STEPS:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify the Order**

  - > Confirm price and quantity for all items.

  - > Ensure signed quotes are attached (required for doors and frames).

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Select Address**

  - > Choose the saved warehouse address.

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Review Shipping & ETA**

  - > Confirm the ETA for each item.

  - > Validate shipping and packing charges.

  - > Flag any items with a long ETA or backorder status.

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Place the Order**

  - > Submit the order.

  - > Copy the vendor confirmation number.

  - > Update the internal system immediately.

**2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - EXPEDITED DROPSHIP**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify the Order**

  - > Confirm price and quantity for all items.

  - > Ensure signed quotes are attached (required for doors and frames).

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Enter Address**

  - > Enter the customer’s shipping address.

  - > For oversized items, add the FedEx account.

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Review Shipping & ETA**

  - > Confirm the ETA for each item.

  - > Validate shipping and packing charges.

  - > Flag any items with a long ETA or backorder status.

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Place the Order**

  - > Submit the order.

  - > Copy the vendor confirmation number.

  - > Update the internal system immediately.
