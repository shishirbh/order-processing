# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Quickserv vendor orders are processed through email via Backoffice only.

  - > All Quikserv orders are dropshipped ONLY.

  - > There is no minimum order quantity (MOQ).

  - > There are no Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements for this vendor.

  - > ALWAYS replace vendor email with: <span class="underline">orders@quikserv.com</span> every time. Sales email must NEVER be used.

# **2.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### **When to Use:**

  - > Small / standard items

  - > No freight indicators

  - > No special handling requirements

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Use Back Office Email**

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Replace vendor email with** orders@quikserv.com

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Send standard draft**

**2.1.5 Step 5 —** **Await confirmation**

Purchasing Team

**2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - FREIGHT/SHIPPING SENSITIVE ITEMS**

**Freight Identification (If ANY apply → Freight):**

  - > Oversized

  - > Bulk quantity

  - > Heavy items

  - > Glass products

  - > Palletized shipments

  - > Canada shipments (often freight)

⚠️ **Never assume non-freight** if unsure

### **Freight Approval Rule (MANDATORY):**

  - > **DO NOT PROCESS** without shipping cost approval

  - > Shipping cost must be approved by **Marina**

### **STEPS:**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 —** **Identify item as freight**

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Provide Marina with:**

  - > Vendor Address:  
    > **11441 Brittmoore Park Dr., Houston, TX 77041  
    > **

  - > Item details

  - > Click Place Order.

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Wait for Marina’s approval**

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Only after approval → send dropship email**

🚫**Always Avoid:**

  - > Sending dropship email before freight approval

  - > Estimating shipping cost yourself

### **2.2.5 Standard Dropship Email (MANDATORY)**

Use **exactly** this draft:

Subject: Dropship Order – \[Order Number\]

Hi,

Please ship this order directly to our customer.  
Kindly let us know if any changes.

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

**2.3 PROCESS TO REQUEST LARGE / OVERSIZED ITEMS - SHIPPING DIMENSIONS**

### **When This Step Is Required:**

  - > Large quantity orders

  - > Bulky SKUs

  - > Pallet-suspected items

  - > Any case where dimensions are unknown

### **Mandatory Vendor Dimension Check:**

### → Before Marina can calculate freight, dimensions must come from Quikserv.

### **STEPS:**

### **2.3.1 Step 1 — Email Quikserv requesting dimensions**

### **2.3.2 Step 2 — Wait for vendor response**

### **2.3.3 Step 3 — Forward dimensions to Marina**

### **2.3.4 Step 4 — Marina calculates & approves shipping**

### **2.3.5 Step 5 — Only then** 

### → process the order → send standard dropship email

### **2.3.6 Dimension Request Email (MANDATORY)**

Subject: Shipping Dimensions Request – \[Order Number\]

Hi,

Could you please let us know the shipping dimensions for the item below?

AM12X12MBL : 41 Units (example)

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

**2.4 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - HIGH-RISK / HIGH-CONTROL ITEMS**

## → Special Items include — California Window / Level 3 Bulletproof Window

### **Mandatory Requirements (ALL REQUIRED)**

  - > Vendor Quote

  - > Glass Thickness Details

  - > Customer-Signed Quote

  - > Shipping Approval

**STEPS:**

### **2.4.1 Step 1 — Forward order to Sales Team**

### **2.4.2 Step 2 — Sales obtains vendor quote**

### **2.4.3 Step 3 — Customer signs the quote**

### **2.4.4 Step 4 — Shipping approval obtained**

### **2.4.5 Step 5 — Only after all above → Purchasing processes order** 

### **Email Rules for Special Items:**

  - > Use **Purchasing Email**

  - > Use **standard dropship draft**

Subject line MUST include:  
Convert Quote – \[Quote Number\]

  - > Attach:
    
      - > Vendor Quote
    
      - > Vendor Purchase Order

🚫**Always Avoid:**

  - > Processing without signed customer quote

  - > Missing glass thickness details

  - > Skipping Sales involvement

**2.4 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - CANADA ORDERS - FREIGHT ITEMS**

### **When This Applies:**

  - > Destination = Canada

  - > Item requires freight shipping

### **Additional Mandatory Steps:**

### **2.4.1 Step 1 — Forward order to Sales Team**

### **2.4.2 Step 2 — Sales obtains broker details**

### **2.4.3 Step 3 — Justin approves shipping**

### **2.4.4 Step 4 — Purchasing sends dropship email**

### **2.4.5 Step 5 — Broker details MUST be included in email**

### **Email Rules:**

  - > Use standard dropship draft

  - > Add broker information clearly

  - > Use Purchasing Email (BO) only

🚫 **Always Avoid:**

  - > Processing Canada freight without broker details

  - > Skipping Justin’s approval
