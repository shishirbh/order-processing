# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > True Value orders are processed via the vendor website.

  - > Weekly Order Minimum (MOV) is $1,000.

  - > Orders are submitted daily, vendor has the submission day on Tuesday and Thursday

  - > Always copy vendor confirmation numbers.

  - > Never mix in-house and dropship items in one vendor order.

### **Always Avoid:**

❌ Ordering from RDC 41 when item is available in 04  
❌ Ignoring Received Partial or Ship Later status  
❌ Dropshipping hazmat items  
❌ Skipping freight charge verification  
❌ Processing restricted items without review

## **1.4 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

## → Dropship is allowed with conditions.

### **1.4.1 Dropship MAY be used when:**

  - ## Item is unavailable in Warehouse 04 but available in another warehouse

  - ## Item qualifies as **Small Parcel = YES**

  - ## Shipping size and quantity are practical

### **1.4.2 Dropship NOT allowed when:**

  - ## **Item is Hazmat** → must be ordered in-house

  - ## Small Parcel eligibility applies only to 4 warehouses, therefore Hazmat dropship is restricted

  - ## Freight or oversized items require in-house handling or special approval.

  - > **Do not dropship freight items from Corsicana**

## ⚠️ Hazmat dropship is restricted due to warehouse eligibility limits.

**1.4.3 Dropship for 2 Business Day orders is allowed only from**:

  - > Harvard (01)

  - > Hanover (27)

  - > Woodland (13)

## **1.5 Handling Out-of-Stock & Discontinued Items**

**1.5.1 Out-of-Stock Orders:**

  - > Review daily.

  - > If restocked → process normally.

  - > If ETA \> 1 month → inform customer.

  - > If no ETA → check alternate vendors.

  - > Move to an alternate vendor if price difference is minimal.

  - > Out-of-stock seasonal items cannot be processed.

### **1.5.2 Ship Later Status:**

→ Indicates backorder from DK Hardware.

Handling Rules:

  - > If status = *Can Be Filled / On the Way* → do not remove.

  - > If status = *Open* → check other warehouses.

  - > If unavailable → move to Out-of-Stock order.

### **1.5.3 Not Stocked in Main Warehouse (04):**

→ Item will not return to main warehouse.

  - > Check other warehouses.

  - > If ETA \> 1 month → inform customer.

### **1.6 ETA – True Value Main (04)**

  - > Orders processed on **Tuesday** → Delivered **Friday**.

  - > Orders processed on **Thursday** → Delivered **Monday**.

  - > Also check there is no item left behind in the cart after placing the order

**1.8 Freight / Special Item Rules**

Freight or oversized items require special handling:

  - > BOL must be provided when dropshipping freight items

  - > Do not dropship freight from **Corsicana**.

  - > Verify shipping costs before processing.

  - > Watch for fraud risk and cost anomalies.

  - > **Hazmat Freight** - Must be ordered **in-house only**.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**Eligibility:**

  - > Used when item is available in Warehouse 04.

**Steps:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Item Details**

Confirm:

  - > Item code

  - > Description

  - > Image

  - > Price

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Ensure items are added to Warehouse 04 only.

  - > Avoid adding to Central / RDC 41.

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Pricing Verification**

  - > Verify price while adding each item.

  - > Update pricing in Vendor Order.

→ Mismatch may cause cart total discrepancies.

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Freight & Shipping Review**

  - > Verify shipping charges for freight items.

  - > Items up to 10 ft ship via truck delivery (no additional fee).

**2.1.5 Step 5 — Restricted Item Review**

  - > Check item notes carefully.

  - > If restricted:

<!-- end list -->

  - > Inform customer

  - > Move order to Problem

<!-- end list -->

  - > Examples: fertilizer, medicine cannot be shipped to Canada

<!-- end list -->

  - > Request a U.S. address from the customer if applicable.

**2.1.6 Step 6 — Cart Validation**

  - > Confirm Cart Total = VO Total.

**2.1.7 Step 7 — Checkout Process**

  - > Enter PO number (without “DKH”)

  - > Shipping Method: Add to my regular warehouse daily

  - > Copy cart total into VO notes

  - > Place order

**2.1.8 Step 8 — Post Submission**

  - > Copy the Order Confirmation Number.

  - > Paste it into the Vendor Order notes

### **  
2.2 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — DROPSHIP** 

**Eligibility:**

  - > Item unavailable in Warehouse 04

  - > Available in another warehouse

  - > Small Parcel = YES

  - > Not hazmat

**STEPS:**

**2.2.1 Step 1** — **Verify Alternate Warehouse Availability**

  - > Confirm stock in another True Value warehouse..

**2.2.2 Step 2 —** **Confirm Small Parcel Eligibility**

  - > Dropship allowed only if Small Parcel = YES.

**2.2.3 Step 3 —** **Hazmat Verification**

  - > If item is hazmat → STOP → order in-house.

**2.2.4 Step 4 — Evaluate Size & Quantity**

  - > Confirm dropship practicality.

**2.2.5 Step 5 —** **Submit Order**

  - > Proceed with dropship from eligible warehouse.

**2.2.6 Step 6 — Record Confirmation**

  - > Copy confirmation number into vendor order record.
