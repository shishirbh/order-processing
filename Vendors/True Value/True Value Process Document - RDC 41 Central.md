# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > This warehouse caters to In-House Orders ONLY

  - > Weekly Order Minimum (MOV) is $1,000.

### **1.1.1 Submission Schedule:**

  - > The Vendor Order (VO) for Central Warehouse (RDC 41) is placed every Thursday.

  - > The Central order is processed only after the Main Warehouse (04) order is submitted.

### **1.1.2 Warehouse Rules:**

  - > Order only items available in Central Warehouse (RDC 41).

  - > Dropship is allowed from Central when required and applicable.

### **1.1.3 Processing Platform:**

  - > All orders are processed through the True Value website.

**1.1.4 ETA — Central Warehouse (RDC 41)**

  - > Standard ETA: 7 days

  - > Orders placed Thursday → delivered by the following Thursday

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**Step-by-Step Process — Central Warehouse (RDC 41):**

### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Item Verification**

### Verify on the website:

  - ### Item code

  - ### Description

  - ### Image

  - ### Price

### **2.1.2 Step 2 — Warehouse Selection**

  - > Ensure items are added only to RDC 41.

  - > Confirm the warehouse displays:

  - > “Warehouse: Central – Shopped from RDC 41”

### **2.1.3 Step 3 — Item Addition Rules**

  - > Add items from the main item page only.

  - > Items shown on the secondary page cannot be added to the RDC 41 cart.

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Price Verification**

  - > Verify pricing while adding each item.

  - > Update pricing in the Vendor Order (VO).

  - > Any mismatch may cause cart total discrepancies at checkout.

### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Freight Items**

  - > Verify shipping charges before adding freight items.

  - > Items up to 10 ft can ship via truck with no additional fee.

### **2.1.6 Step 6 — Restricted Items**

  - > Review restriction notes on the item page.

### **If restricted:**

  - > Inform the customer

  - > Move the order to Problem

  - > Examples: fertilizer, medicine (cannot ship to Canada)  
    > → Request a U.S. address if required.

### **2.1.7 Step 7 — Cart Validation**

### After adding all items:

  - > Confirm VO Total = Cart Total.

  - > Verify all items are assigned to RDC 41 only**.**

### **2.1.8 Step 8 — Checkout Process**

  - > Click Checkout.

  - > Enter the PO Number (without “DKH”).

  - > Copy the cart total into VO Notes.

  - > Shipping Method: Add to ***my regular warehouse daily***.

  - > Place the order.

### **2.1.9 Step 9 — Post-Submission**

  - > Copy the Order Confirmation Number.

  - > Paste it into Vendor Order notes.
