**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Adding Items (Quick Order Tab Method)**

  - > Log in to the Hager Vendor Website.

  - > Go to the Quick Order tab.

  - > Paste or manually enter the item code(s).

  - > Enter the required quantity for each item.

  - > Click Add to Cart.
    
      - > *Note:* Clicking on an item to view details may show limited or no information — this is normal.

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Cart & Checkout Process**

  - > Open Cart

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Correct item codes
    
      - > Correct quantities

  - > Verify order total ≥ $1,500

  - > Select Partial Ship Order
    
      - > Hager allows partial shipments since stock is unknown

  - > Enter Vendor PO Number
    
      - > Must match Backoffice PO

  - > Submit the order

**Always Required:  
**✅ Partial Ship Order  
✅ Vendor PO number entered

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Post-Submission Expectations**

  - > Stock confirmation → Unknown until Hager processes

  - > Backorders → Possible

  - > ETA → Not provided

  - > Dropship → Never

**If Backorder Occurs:**

  - > Wait for Hager communication

  - > Do not cancel or modify unless instructed
