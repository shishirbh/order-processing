# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Poolcorp vendor orders are processed directly on the vendor website.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements for this vendor is $150.00.Customer orders do not have any MOV requirement.

  - > Fulfillment Type for all orders is In-House Only.

  - > Dropship orders are not allowed.

  - > Expedited orders are allowed only if the item is in local stock. Otherwise, inform customer of ground-only shipping.

  - > Items marked as “Obsolete” on the vendor website are considered discontinued. These items must not be ordered. The customer should be notified and informed of alternate items.

  - > Item assignment depends on warehouse availability:

> → In Stock (warehouses listed in Section 1.2) → B7 vendor order
> 
> → Out of Stock → POOLCORP Out of Stock Order

### **1.2 Stock Verification**

→ Always check stock on the POOLCORP website.  
  
→ If the item is in stock in any of these warehouses, assign it to the **B7 vendor order**:

  - > 166 – Medley, FL

  - > 231 – NPT Pompano, FL

  - > C6 – Bird Road, FL

  - > 144 – Delray Beach, FL

  - > 264 – Royal Palm Beach, FL

  - > D4 – West Palm Beach, FL

  - > 54 – Port St. Lucie, FL

  - > B7 – Fort Lauderdale, FL

→ If the item is **out of stock** or not available in these warehouses, assign it to the **POOLCORP Out of Stock Order**.

### **1.3 Price Verification**

  - > Small price differences (a few cents or up to a dollar or two) between the vendor website price and the customer order price are **acceptable**.

  - > If the price difference is **significant**, check with **Elina** before proceeding.
