# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > Groves Inc. orders are processed by Back Office email only.

  - > All orders are dropshipped.

  - > There is no minimum order quantity.

  - > Orders must always be sent to: orderentry@groves.com

  - > Due to freight-heavy products, shipping approval is required for most orders.

  - > Monitor inbox after submission for:  
    > → confirmations  
    > → shipping readiness notices  
    > → dimension clarifications

  - > Respond promptly to prevent delays.

### **Always Avoid:**

❌ Sending freight orders without approval  
❌ Allowing shipment before BOL is issued  
❌ Sending orders without confirming shipping details  
❌ Skipping dimension verification for bulky items

## **1.2 Mandatory Order Classification**

Before processing, classify the order:

**Shipping Type:**

  - > Standard parcel (rare)

  - > Freight / oversized / heavy

**Shipment Size:**

  - > Small / standard

  - > Large / bulky / palletized

Most Groves Inc. products fall under **freight classification**.

**SECTION 2 — EMAIL TEMPLATES**

## **2.1 Standard Dropship Email (Non-Freight Items)**

If an item is small enough to ship normally (rare but possible), it can be processed immediately.

### **Mandatory Checks Before Sending:**

  - > Confirm customer shipping address

  - > Confirm item quantity

  - > Confirm SKU and description

**EMAIL:**

Hi,

Please ship this order directly to our customer.

Kindly let us know if any changes.

Regards,

Purchasing Team

\------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## **2.2 Dropship Email for Freight Items**

Most Groves Inc. items are large and heavy, so they must be treated as freight

**EMAIL:**

Hi,

Please ship this order directly to our customer.  
Please let us know once the order is ready.  
We will provide you a bill of lading.

Kindly let us know if any changes.

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

### **Critical Rules:  
❌** Shipment must NOT move until:

  - > Order-ready confirmation received

  - > BOL is issued and shared

\------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**2.3 Checking Shipping Dimensions**

Whenever an item is large, bulky, or high quantity, request shipping dimensions from the vendor before sending anything to Marina.

**EMAIL:**

Hi,

Could you please let us know the Shipping Dimension for the item below?

AM12X12MBL : 1 Units (Example)

Regards,

Purchasing Team

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Create Vendor Order**

  - > Create the vendor order in Backoffice.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Verify Customer Address**

  - > Confirm accuracy before submission.

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Submit by Email**

  - > Send using the **Standard Dropship Template**.

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Monitor Vendor Response**

  - > Watch for confirmation or changes.

**2.3 PROCESSING DROPSHIP ORDERS — LARGE / FREIGHT ITEMS**

**3.2.1 Step 1 — Confirm Shipping Details**

  - > If not already known, ask the vendor for:
    
      - > Shipping dimensions
    
      - > Weight

  - > Use the shipping-dimension email (see Section 2.3).

**3.2.2 Step 2 — Send Info to Marina**

  - > Provide Marina:
    
      - > Shipping dimensions
    
      - > Weight
    
      - > Vendor ship-from address  
        > **818 Trakk Ln, Woodstock, IL 60098**

**3.2.3 Step 3 — Wait for Marina’s Approval**

  - > Do not send the order until Marina confirms shipping is approved.

**3.2.4 Step 4 — Process the Order**

  - > Once approved, send the freight dropship email.

**SECTION 4 — DECISION MATRIX**

Table 1 - Groves Inc. Processing Decision Matrix

| **Scenario**              | **Condition**    | **Action**                           |
| ------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Small parcel item         | Standard size    | Dropship immediately                 |
| Large / heavy item        | Freight required | Request dimensions → Marina approval |
| Freight shipment approved | BOL required     | Send freight template & provide BOL  |
| Shipping details unknown  | Any              | Request dimensions first             |
