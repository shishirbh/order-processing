### **1.2 Minimum Order & Fees**

### **1.2.1 Minimum Order Value Rule**

  - > MOV = $150

  - > If PO subtotal \< $150 → Lavi applies a small order fee.

  - > No exception for free shipping (vendor does not provide free shipping).

### **1.2.2 Cutting Charges**

  - > Cutting fee = $10 per cut.

  - > Always calculate & add to PO before sending.

  - > Example: 10 cuts × $10 = $100 cutting charge.

**Always confirm:**

  - > Number of cuts.

  - > Whether cuts are identical or different.

  - > Customer’s approval in case cutting charges significantly increase cost.

**Avoid trap:  
** → Do NOT assume cutting is free. Always calculate and include.

### **1.3 PRICING CONFIRMATION RULE (CRITICAL)**

Lavi pricing does not change frequently, but always wait for price confirmation before proceeding. (Updated: 2026-04-26) Mandatory Pricing Workflow:

  - > Submit the PO via email to Xavier.

  - > Wait for price confirmation (revised pricing).

  - > Update DK PO EXACTLY as per Lavi’s final prices.

  - > Email the revised PO back to Xavier.

DK has separate pricing for in-house and dropship orders. The Backoffice shows in-house pricing. Use the correct price set based on order type when submitting the initial PO to Xavier. (Updated: 2026-04-26)

→ **If revised PO is NOT sent back → Lavi WILL NOT ship.**

**Example:**

  - > You send PO for $680 based on DK pricing.

  - > Xavier responds with an updated total $712.

  - > You must update the DK PO to $712 and resend.

  - > Only then will order move to fulfillment.
