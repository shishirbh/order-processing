**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Order Status**

  - > Confirm the customer order status is “New”.

  - > Proceed only if Create PO option is available in backoffice.

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Stock Confirmation**

  - > Verify all items are in stock on the Häfele website.

  - > If any item is out of stock, do NOT proceed with PO creation.

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Access Backoffice**

  - > Navigate to Backoffice → Create PO.

  - > Select In-House as the fulfillment method.

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Address Selection**

  - > Use the saved warehouse address for shipping.

  - > Do not enter the customer’s address for in-house orders.

**2.1.5 Step 5 — PO Details**

  - > Enter the correct PO number.

  - > Confirm all item quantities and pricing match the customer order.

**2.1.6 Step 6** **— Final Review**

Verify the following before submission:

  - > All items are in stock

  - > Correct warehouse address

  - > Correct PO number

  - > Quantities and pricing are accurate

**2.1.7 Step 7** **— Submit PO**

  - > Submit the PO in backoffice.

  - > Copy the Häfele reference number.

**2.1.8 Step 8** **— Documentation**

  - > Paste the Häfele reference number into the order record immediately.

### **Always Avoid:**

  - > Creating a PO when order status is not “New”

  - > Proceeding without stock confirmation

<!-- end list -->

  - > Entering customer address for in-house orders

# **2.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — EXPEDITED DROPSHIP 1- BUSINESS DAY (Next Day)**

**→ Backoffice PO Creation (No Website Ordering)**

**2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify Order Status**

  - > Confirm the customer order status is “New”.

  - > Proceed only if Create PO option is available in backoffice.

**2.2.2 Step 2 — Stock Confirmation**

  - > Verify all items are in stock on the Häfele website.

  - > If any item is out of stock, do NOT proceed with PO creation.

**2.2.3 Step 3 — Access Backoffice**

  - > Navigate to Backoffice → Create PO.

  - > Select Dropship as the fulfillment method.

**2.2.4 Step 4 — Address Selection**

  - > Enter the customer’s shipping address.

  - > Ensure address accuracy before proceeding.

**2.2.5 Step 5 — Shipping Method Selection**

  - > Select Fedex Next Day shipping.

  - > Review the Next Day shipping cost.

**2.2.6 Step 6 — Cost Evaluation**

  - > If shipping cost is within the customer-paid amount (+/- a few dollars) → proceed.

  - > If shipping cost is significantly higher → inform the customer and obtain approval before proceeding.

**2.2.7 Step 7 — PO Details**

  - > Enter the Dropship Order PO number.

  - > Confirm item quantities and pricing match the customer order.

**2.2.8 Step 8 — Final Review**

Verify the following before submission:

  - > Items and quantities

  - > Customer shipping address

  - > Shipping method (Next Day)

  - > Shipping cost approval (if applicable)

**2.2.9 Step 9 — Submit PO**

  - > Submit the PO in backoffice.

  - > Copy the Häfele reference number.

**Always Avoid:**

  - > Placing next-day orders without verifying shipping cost

  - > Absorbing large shipping cost differences without approval

  - > Creating a PO when order status is not “New”

**2.3 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — EXPEDITED DROPSHIP 2- BUSINESS DAY**

**→ Backoffice PO Creation (No Website Ordering)**

**Steps:**

**2.3.1 Step 1 — Verify Order Status**

  - > Confirm the customer order status is “New”.

  - > Proceed only if Create PO option is available in backoffice.

**2.3.2 Step 2 — Stock Confirmation**

  - > Verify all items are in stock on the Häfele website.

  - > If any item is out of stock, do NOT proceed with PO creation.

**2.3.3 Step 3 — Access Backoffice**

  - > Navigate to Backoffice → Create PO.

  - > Select Dropship as the fulfillment method.

**2.3.4 Step 4 — Address Selection**

  - > Enter the customer’s shipping address.

  - > Ensure address accuracy before proceeding.

**2.3.5 Step 5 — Shipping Method Selection**

  - > Select Fedex 2nd Day Air as the shipping fulfillment method.

### **2.3.6 Step 6 — ETA Validation**

Review ETA in backoffice:

  - > ETA = 2–3 business days → proceed as standard ground dropship

  - > ETA longer than 3 business days → do NOT process as expedited

### **2.3.7 Step 7 — PO Details**

  - > Enter a **placeholder PO number** (as per internal convention).

  - > Confirm pricing and quantities.

### **2.3.8 Step 8 — Final Review**

Verify:

  - > Items and quantities

  - > Customer address

  - > Shipping method (Standard Ground)

  - > ETA eligibility

### **2.3.9 Step 9 — Submit PO**

  - > Submit the PO in backoffice.

  - > Copy the Häfele reference number.

| ETA \> 1 month         | Any                      | Inform customer            |
