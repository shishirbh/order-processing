# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > All orders are processed by email only via Backoffice.

  - > There is no Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirement.

  - > Dropship is NOT supported.

  - > Stock Verification is MANDATORY before any order processing

## **Always Avoid:**

❌ Processing without verification  
❌Guessing item availability  
❌Using website or external assumptions  
❌Processing partial orders without approva

⚠️ **Critical Principle:  
** NO order may be processed unless stock is verified using approved methods discussed in Section 1.2.

### **1.2 Approved Stock Verification Methods**

Stock can be confirmed only using one of the two methods below:

##### **1.2.1 Method 1: Backoffice (BO) Stock Tab**

  - > Path: BO → Product → Stock Tab

  - > Review current stock associated with Imperial Dade

  - > Use this as the primary verification method

##### **1.2.2 Method 2: Recent Shipped Imperial Dade Orders**

  - > Review recent Imperial Dade orders

  - > Orders must be:
    
      - > Shipped
    
      - > Paid
    
      - > Delivered successfully

  - > Product must match exact SKU / part number

⚠️ **Important Rule:  
** If stock cannot be verified using **either method**, the order **MUST NOT** be processed.

**1.2.3 What is NOT Allowed**

❌ **Do NOT** check stock on Imperial Dade’s website

❌ **Do NOT** assume availability based on past memory  
❌ **Do NOT** process orders hoping stock will be available later

## **1.3 Exceptions & Edge Case Handling**

#### **Case 1: Stock Unclear or Conflicting**

  - > BO shows no stock, but old order shipped months ago  
    > → Treat as NOT VERIFIED → Do NOT process

#### **Case 2: Multiple Items, Partial Stock**

  - > Some items verified, others not  
    > → Do NOT split or partially process**  
    > ** → Escalate for guidance

#### **Case 3: High-Value or Bulk Orders**

  - > Even if large quantity, same rules apply**  
    > ** → Stock verification is mandatory

## **1.4 Escalation Rules**

Escalate to a senior or vendor manager if:

  - > Stock cannot be verified

  - > Data between BO and past orders conflicts

  - > Order is time-sensitive but stock is unclear

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Identify Order Type**

  - > Confirm order is Standard Ground

  - > Confirm vendor = Imperial Dade

  - > Confirm order is In-House eligible

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Perform Stock Verification**

  - > Use BO Stock Tab OR

  - > Review recent shipped Imperial Dade orders

**Example:**

  - > Product SKU shows stock in BO → ✅ Proceed

  - > Product shipped successfully last week → ✅ Proceed

  - > No BO stock + no recent shipped history → ❌ STOP

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Assign to Incoming In-House Vendor Order**

  - > Once stock is confirmed:
    
      - > Assign item(s) to Incoming In-House Vendor Order
    
      - > Follow standard internal purchasing workflow
