# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Poolcorp vendor orders are processed directly on the vendor website.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) of $500 applies to vendor orders only (not customer orders).

  - > Fulfillment Type for all orders is In-House Only

  - > Dropship orders are not allowed.

  - > Expedited orders are allowed only if the item is in local stock. Otherwise, inform customer of ground-only shipping.

  - > No discontinued item rules apply.

### **1.2 Stock Locations**

→ FWP has multiple warehouses. When processing customer orders, items are assigned based on availability in the following warehouses:

  - > Main Warehouse (OPA LOCKA)

  - > Additional FWP warehouses as listed on the vendor website (when applicable).

### **1.3 Stock Assignment**

  - > When processing a customer order, verify stock availability in all FWP warehouses.

  - > If the item is in stock in any of the warehouses, assign the item to that warehouse.

  - > If the item is out of stock in all warehouses, assign it to the OPA LOCKA main warehouse for processing.
