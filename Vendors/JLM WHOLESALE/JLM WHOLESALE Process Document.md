# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > JLM vendor orders are processed via Vendor Website only.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements:

<!-- end list -->

  - > $2,000 → Vendor In-House Orders.

  - > $350 → Lightweight / items below 36 inches.

  - > No MOV → Dropship orders for:
    
      - > Heavy or lengthy items (\>36 inches).
    
      - > Expedited orders (1- or 2-Business Day) if item is in stock.

<!-- end list -->

  - > Dropship is supported under circumstances discussed in Section 1.2.

  - > Default Vendor Shipping Method is Standard Ground.

  - > All expedited orders → Dropship only.

  - > FedEx Account \#: 632647611.

  - > Always copy vendor confirmation number.

  - > Never mix dropship and in-house items in one vendor order.

## **Always Avoid:**

❌ Dropshipping lightweight non-expedited items  
❌ Missing FedEx note when required  
❌ Using DK address for dropship orders  
❌ Processing expedited orders as in-house  
❌ Ignoring MOV checks

### **1.2 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

### **1.2.1 Dropship is ALLOWED when:**

  - > Item Length \> 36 inches (heavy or lengthy)

  - > Customer requests expedited shipping
    
      - > 1-Business Day
    
      - > 2-Business Day

  - > Item is confirmed in stock

### **1.2.2 Dropship MOV Rules**

  - > Heavy / Lengthy items → No MOV

  - > Expedited orders → No MOV if in stock

  - > Regular lightweight items → Do NOT dropship unless expedited

⚠️ **Always confirm stock before processing any dropship order.  
→** If item is not in stock → item will be backordered

## **1.3 Handling Discontinued Items**

### **Identification:**

Item shows -

  - > “Item Not Found”

  - > Error page

  - > Removed SKU

### **Action Steps:**

  - > Stop processing

  - > Notify customer item is discontinued

  - > Manually find alternatives in other vendors like Seclock, Top-Notch, Banner Solutions, IMLSSS, etc.

  - > Confirm stock

  - > Offer alternative to customer

❌ Never substitute without customer approval.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **Eligibility:**

  - > Used for non-expedited, non-lengthy items

  - > MOV rules apply

**STEPS:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 —Add Items to Cart**

  - > Add all order items

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Shipping Address**

  - > ❌ Do NOT check “The New Ship To”

  - > Select DK Hardware Supply address from dropdown

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Shipping Method**

  - > Select Ground

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Check MOV**

  - > ≥ $2,000
    
      - > No FedEx note required

  - > \< $2,000

**2.1.5 Step 5 —** Add note:  
  
“ PLEASE SHIP USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#63264761 ”

**2.1.6 Step 6 —** **Payment**

  - > PO Number

  - > Net 30 Days

**2.1.7 Step 7 —** **Final Review & Submit**

  - > Place order

  - > Record confirmation number

### **Example:**

  - > $2,450 order shipping to DK → In-House, no FedEx note

  - > $1,200 order shipping to DK → In-House, FedEx note required

**2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER - EXPEDITED DROPSHIP ORDERS**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Confirm item is in stock.

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Proceed to Address & Shipping Page**

  - > Click Place Order.

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Enter Customer Address**

  - > Check “The New Ship To”

  - > Enter customer address.

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Select Shipping + Notes**

#### **Option 1: 1-Business Day**

  - > Shipping: UPS Next Day

  - > Order Note:

“PLEASE SHIP 1 BUSINESS DAY USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611”

#### **Option 2: 2-Business Day**

  - > Shipping: Ground

  - > Order Note:

“PLEASE SHIP 2 BUSINESS DAY USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611”

### **2.2.5 Step 5 — Payment**

  - > PO Number

  - > Net 30 Days

### **2.2.6 Step 6 — Final Review & Submit**

  - > Place order

  - > Record confirmation number

### **Example:**

  - > Customer selects Next Day Air → Dropship required, no MOV, FedEx note mandatory.

**2.3 PROCESSING DROPSHIP ORDERS - HEAVY/LENGTHY ITEMS (\>36”)**

### **2.3.1 Step 1 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Add only the eligible heavy/lengthy items

### **2.3.2 Step 2 — Proceed to Address & Shipping Page**

  - > Click Place Order.

### **2.3.3 Step 3 — Enter Customer Address**

  - > Check “The New Ship To”

  - > Enter customer address.

### **2.3.4 Step 4 — Select Shipping Method**

  - > Standard orders → Ground

  - > Expedited:
    
      - > 1-Business Day → UPS Next Day
    
      - > 2-Business Day → Ground

### **2.3.5 Step 5 — Payment**

  - > PO Number

  - > Net 30 Days

  - > Click Continue

### **2.3.6 Step 6 — Insert Order Notes (Mandatory)  
  
**“PLEASE SHIP USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611”

### **2.2.7 Step 7 — Final Review & Submit**

  - > Verify items, address, shipping method, PO

  - > Place order

  - > Copy confirmation number into vendor order system

### **Example:**

  - > 84" aluminum extrusion, standard shipping → Dropship allowed, no MOV, FedEx note required.
