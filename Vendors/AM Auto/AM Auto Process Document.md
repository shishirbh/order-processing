# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - ### All AM Auto orders are dropshipped directly from the vendor.

  - ### Orders are processed via Back Office email only.

  - ### Vendor contact email: sales.amauto.co@gmail.com

  - ### There is no in-house fulfillment for AM Auto items.

  - ### Always include:  
    → customer shipping address  
    → item quantity  
    → SKU & description  
    → any special shipping requirements

  - > For Freight Items, we do not provide a Bill of Lading (BOL). All shipments must be sent using AM Auto’s shipping account only.

### **1.2 Handling Charges**

Handling fees are applied per item and are in addition to shipping costs.

### **Handling Fee Structure:**

**1.2.1 Small Boxes — $35  
**Examples:

  - > Nissan NV200

  - > Ram Promaster City

  - > Universal bunk sliders

**1.2.2 Medium Boxes — $40  
**Examples:

  - > Rear doors for large vans

  - > 40x15 bunk sliders

  - > Ford Transit Connect

**1.2.3 Large Windows — $50  
**Examples:

  - > Sprinter

  - > Promaster

  - > Transit

  - > Transit Connect front panels

  - > Metris front panels

### **1.3 Stock & Shipping Costs Confirmation Requirements**

  - > AM Auto provides the shipping cost directly; therefore, requesting shipping dimensions is not required.

  - > Before submitting the order, confirm stock availability and shipping charges with the vendor.

  - > Once confirmed, proceed using the standard email draft to place the order.

  - > Stock and shipping must be confirmed for:

<!-- end list -->

  - > 1-Business-Day orders

  - > 2-Business-Day orders

Always confirm:

✔ Stock availability  
✔ ETA  
✔ Shipping cost

→ Wait for vendor confirmation before processing.

**1.4 Dropship Quantity Rules**

### **1.4.1 Quantity ≤ 100**

  - > Dropship directly

  - > No stock or ETA verification required

### **1.4.2 Quantity \> 100**

  - > Confirm stock availability

  - > Confirm ETA

  - > Confirm pricing & shipping cost from AM Auto

  - > Proceed only after vendor approval

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### → Default method for all AM Auto orders.

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Create Vendor Order**

  - ### Create the vendor order in Backoffice.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Verify Order Details**

### Confirm:

  - ### correct SKU

  - ### quantity

  - ### customer shipping address

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Apply Handling Charges**

  - ### Add applicable handling fee per item.

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Submit Order**

  - ### Send order via Back Office email.

### **3.1.5 Step 5 — Monitor Vendor Response**

  - ### Watch for confirmation or updates.

# **3.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — 1-2 BUSINESS DAY**

**3.2.1 Step 1 — Request ETA & Shipping**

  - > Use the confirmation template.

### **3.2.2 Step 2 — Wait for Vendor Response**

Confirm:

  - > stock availability

  - > ETA

  - > shipping cost

### **3.2.3 Step 3 — Cost Review**

  - > Proceed if cost & ETA are acceptable.

### **3.2.4 Step 4 — Submit Dropship Order**

  - > Send via Back Office email.

# **3.3 PROCESSING LARGE QUANTITY ORDERS**

### **3.3.1 Step 1 — Identify Quantity**

  - > If quantity exceeds 100 units → approval required.

### **3.3.2 Step 2 — Confirm Availability**

Request:

  - > Stock

  - > ETA

  - > Shipping cost

### **3.3.3 Step 3 — Wait for Approval**

  - > Proceed only after vendor confirmation.

### **3.3.4 Step 4 — Submit Order**

  - > Send via Back Office email.

| Expedited order             | 1–2 day delivery | Confirm ETA & cost       | Required                |
| Large quantity              | Qty \> 100       | Vendor approval required | Confirm stock & pricing |
| Small box item              | Any              | Add $35 handling         | Per item                |
