# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

#### **1.1.1 Do’s ✅**

  - > Verify customer & payment details before anything else
    
      - > Cross-check name, address, phone, card using Google / White Pages.
    
      - > If anything looks suspicious → Escalate before processing.

  - > Verify item details on both systems
    
      - > Compare DK Hardware vs ORS NASCO portal:
        
          - > Image, description, specs
        
          - > Price
        
          - > Availability (in stock / out-of-stock)
        
          - > Flags: Special Order (SO), long (≥96"), freight

  - > Respect SO/OFS rules
    
      - > SO/OFS freight → vendor quote required.
    
      - > SO with customization → signed customer quote required.
    
      - > Regular OFS (non-freight) → backorder allowed without quote.

  - > Use stock in this priority
    
      - > Local DKH stock (if economical & available)
    
      - > ORS NASCO – Birmingham warehouse

  - > Lock & document Vendor Orders
    
      - > Change status to Submitted to Vendor.
    
      - > Lock VO after submission.
    
      - > Ensure Cart Total = VO Total.
    
      - > Record in VO notes:
        
          - > Vendor cart total (before submitting)
        
          - > PO number
        
          - > Confirmation number
        
          - > ETA
        
          - > Any backordered lines.

  - > Get shipping approval when needed
    
      - > For freight items and some long items for dropship:
        
          - > Get approval from Marina / Elina before confirming.

#### **1.1.2 Don’ts ❌**

  - > Do not process if:
    
      - > Customer info looks mismatched or suspicious.
    
      - > Card data does not match customer name/address.

  - > Do not ignore SO/OFS flags
    
      - > Never treat SO/OFS as regular stock.
    
      - > Never skip quote/signature requirements for customizable SO.

  - > Do not dropship long items randomly
    
      - > Long items not in Birmingham must follow dropship rules & shipping approval.

  - > Do not submit VO with mismatched totals
    
      - > If Cart Total ≠ VO Total → stop and re-check quantities, prices, replacements.

  - > Do not forget documentation
    
      - > PO number
    
      - > Confirmation number
    
      - > ETA / backorder info
    
      - > Quote emails & signed customer confirmations (for SO).

  - > Do not assign stock randomly
    
      - > Always follow local stock first, then ORS NASCO.
    
      - > Dropship orders must not use local stock in assignment.

  - > Do not dropship freight items without approval
    
      - > Must have explicit OK from Marina / Elina.

### **1.2 Record-Keeping & Audit Notes**

For every ORS NASCO-related order, VO Notes should include:

  - > Cart Total

  - > PO\#

  - > Confirmation \#

  - > ETA

  - > Backorder details

  - > Freight approval notes (who approved, date, cost)

  - > For SO/Quote: reference to customer approval & vendor quote date.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Customer**

  - > Check:
    
      - > Name
    
      - > Shipping & billing address
    
      - > Phone
    
      - > Credit card (if applicable – not Amazon/Zoro/Net30)

  - > Use Google / White Pages or similar.

  - > If mismatch or doubt:
    
      - > Put order On Hold.
    
      - > Escalate to senior/manager.

**Example:**  
Customer: John Smith, address appears as a business, but phone belongs to different state. Card name doesn’t match John Smith → Hold & escalate.

**2.1.2 Step 2 —** **Verify Item**

  - > Open product in DK Hardware & ORS NASCO portal.

  - > Verify:
    
      - > Image matches
    
      - > Description/specs (size, finish, type)
    
      - > Price range is logical
    
      - > Availability: in stock / OFS
    
      - > Flags: SO, freight, long item (≥96")

**Trap to avoid:**  
Similar SKUs with small differences (e.g., -01 vs -02 finish). Always confirm finish/color.

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Special Order (SO)/Out-of-Stock (OFS) Check**

  - > If item is SO (Special Order):
    
      - > Freight OFS (SO + OFS + freight):
        
          - > Request vendor quote.
    
      - > Customer customization present (e.g., special color, drilling pattern):
        
          - > Request signed quote from customer confirming:
            
              - > Part number
            
              - > Price
            
              - > Lead time
            
              - > Non-cancellation / non-return terms.
    
      - > Only after signed confirmation → process order.

  - > If item is OFS but NOT SO:
    
      - > Regular item & OFS:
        
          - > Backorder allowed.
        
          - > No quote needed unless freight.

  - > If item is neither SO nor OFS:
    
      - > Process as regular stock.

**Example:**

  - > SO + customization: customer requests custom length or custom coating → must have signed quote.

  - > OFS but regular: item is temporarily out of stock but standard → just backorder.

**Step 4 requires Elina’s inputs.**

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Warehouse & Long Item Check**

  - > Check length:
    
      - > If item length ≥ 96":
        
          - > Check ORS NASCO – Birmingham availability:
            
              - > If Available in Birmingham → can process In-House.
            
              - > If Not available in Birmingham → order must go as Dropship flow (see Section 2.2 Processing Customer Order — Dropship).

  - > If item is NOT long (length \< 96"):

  - > **Treat as normal.**

**2.1.5 Step 5 —** **Process Incoming Order (In-House)**

  - > Open Process Incoming Order screen.

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Customer verified
    
      - > Item verified

<!-- end list -->

  - > SO/OFS/long item rules understood.

**2.1.6 Step 6 — Assign Products (In-House)**

Rule: Local stock first, then ORS NASCO

  - > If local DKH stock available:
    
      - > Assign quantity from local stock.
    
      - > Remaining (if any) from ORS NASCO (In-House)

  - > If no local stock:
    
      - > Assign entire quantity from ORS NASCO (In-House).

**Status rules:**

  - > Entire order from ORS NASCO only →  
    > Status: Coming In-House.

  - > Part local stock + part ORS NASCO →  
    > Status: Coming In-House with Backorder.

> **Example:  
> **Customer orders 10 pcs.  
> Local stock = 3, ORS NASCO in stock = 20.  
> Assign:

  - > 3 from local

  - > 7 from ORS NASCO → Status Coming In-House with Backorder.

**2.1.7 Step 7 — Create Vendor Order (In-House)**

  - > Use Existing VO (if appropriate) or Create New VO.

  - > Ensure:
    
      - > VO marked as In-House.
    
      - > MOV consideration: target VO total ≥ $1,500.
    
      - > If VO \< $1,500 → hold and club with other in-house items when possible.

**2.1.8 Step 8 — Process Payment**

  - > For normal website orders:
    
      - > Authorize & charge card.

  - > Exceptions (do NOT charge):
    
      - > Amazon
    
      - > Zoro
    
      - > Net30 customers

  - > After payment:
    
      - > Status: New → Coming In-House.

**2.1.9 Step 9 — Finalize**

  - > Click Process Changes.

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > VO number is generated (if new VO).
    
      - > Notes updated with any special handling.

# **2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

**2.2.1 Step 1 —** **Verify Customer**

Same as In-House:

  - > Verify using Google / White Pages.

  - > If mismatch → hold and escalate.

**2.2.2 Step 2 —** **Verify Item**

Same checks as In-House:

  - > DK Hardware vs ORS NASCO (image, specs, price, availability, SO/OFS, freight, long-item).

**2.2.3 Step 3 —** **SO/OFS Check (Dropship)**

Same logic as In-House:

1.  > SO + OFS (especially freight) → vendor quote required.

2.  > SO + customization → must have signed customer quote.

3.  > Regular OFS → backorder; no quote unless freight.

**2.2.4 Step 4 —** **Dropship Eligibility**

  - > Long items (≥96") not available in Birmingham:
    
      - > Eligible for Dropship from ORS NASCO (via FedEx/email).

  - > Freight items:
    
      - > Must get shipping approval from Marina / Elina before dropship.

  - > If local stock or Birmingham in-house is clearly cheaper:
    
      - > Prefer In-House shipment instead of Dropship.

**Always Avoid  
**Dropshipping freight items without checking shipping cost & getting approval.

**2.2.5 Step 5** — **Process Incoming Order (Dropship)**

  - > Open Process Incoming Order screen.

  - > Confirm: Order is approved for dropship (long item or freight with approval, or SO quote).

### **2.2.6 Step 6** — **Assign Products (Dropship)**

  - > For Dropship orders:
    
      - > Assign entire quantity from ORS NASCO.
    
      - > Do not use local stock.

  - > Status:
    
      - > Dropship orders → Placed for Dropship.
    
      - > System color: Red → Green after assignment (per internal system).

**2.2.7 Step 7** — **Create Vendor Order (Dropship)**

  - > Always Create New VO.

  - > Select Dropship option.

  - > Verify shipping address:
    
      - > Dropship only if shipping address is within USA (for SO/quote orders explicitly).

**2.2.8 Step 8 — Process Payment**

  - > Authorize & charge (except Amazon/Zoro/Net30).

  - > Status: New → Placed for Dropship.

**2.2.9 Step 9 — Finalize**

  - > Click Process Changes.

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > VO number generated.
    
      - > Backordered vs ready-to-ship correctly reflected.
    
      - > Notes updated (freight approval details if any).

# **2.3 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.3.1 Step 1 —** **Verify & Lock Vendor Order**

  - > Open VO.

  - > Set status: New → Submitted to Vendor (after verification).

  - > Lock the VO after submission (prevents accidental item additions).

  - > Ensure VO total respects MOV ≥ $1,500:
    
      - > If below → hold and combine with other lines where possible.

**2.3.2 Step 2 —** **Verify Items on VO**

For each item:

  - > Check in DKH system & ORS NASCO portal:
    
      - > SO / OFS
    
      - > Long item (≥96")
    
      - > Freight
    
      - > Backordered lines

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Correct part number
    
      - > Quantity
    
      - > Price
    
      - > Replacement SKUs if any suggested by vendor.

**2.3.3 Step 3 —Quote Criteria (VO)**

  - > If SO & OFS:
    
      - > Request vendor quote (if not already taken at CX stage).

  - > If SO with customer customization:
    
      - > Confirm signed customer quote exists.

  - > If not SO:
    
      - > Process normally unless freight cost looks abnormal.

**2.3.4 Step 4 —** **Add Items to Vendor Cart**

  - > In ORS NASCO portal:
    
      - > Add items to cart one by one.

  - > Compare:
    
      - > Cart Total (portal) vs VO Total (system).

  - > If mismatch:
    
      - > Check:
        
          - > Quantity
        
          - > Price differences
        
          - > Replacements or discontinued lines.

  - > Once matched:
    
      - > Copy Cart Total into VO Notes.

**2.3.5 Step 5** — **Checkout & Submit**

  - > Add PO Number in ORS NASCO checkout.

  - > Re-verify:
    
      - > Cart Total
    
      - > Shipping method (freight / ground)

  - > Submit VO.

### **2.3.6 Step 6** — **Confirm Vendor Order**

  - > After submission:
    
      - > Note Confirmation Number.
    
      - > Note ETA for each line (or shipment).
    
      - > Note Backordered vs ready-to-ship items.

  - > Update VO Notes:
    
      - > “Conf\# XXXXX, ETA XX/XX/XXXX, Backordered: \[items\].”

**2.3.7 Step 7** — **Vendor Order Complete**

A VO is complete when:

  - > All items verified.

  - > Cart Total = VO Total.

  - > Confirmation & ETA recorded.

  - > MOV satisfied or exception approved.

# **2.4 PROCESSING SPECIAL ORDERS/QUOTE ORDERS — DROPSHIP**

**2.4.1 Step 1 —** **Shipping Address Check**

  - > Dropship only if shipping address is within USA.

  - > If not within USA → escalate / consider in-house alternative.

**2.4.2 Step 2 —** **Vendor Quote Email Trail**

  - > Vendor sends quote to purchasing email.

  - > Save the quote thread (will be reused for converting quote into order).

**2.4.3 Step 3 —** **Verify Quote vs Customer Order**

  - > Check:
    
      - > Part number(s)
    
      - > Rate/price
    
      - > Quantity
    
      - > Lead time
    
      - > Additional charges (freight, handling, setup)

  - > Quote terms like non-cancellable, non-returnable must be visible.

**2.4.4 Step 4 —** **Communicate with Customer**

  - > Inform customer about:
    
      - > Part number, quantity, price
    
      - > Lead time
    
      - > Non-cancellation / non-return terms
    
      - > Any additional charges

  - > Get explicit customer agreement (email approval or signed quote).

**2.4.5 Step 5 —** **If Quote Matches Order & Customer Agrees**

  - > Process customer order in system:
    
      - > Create / update CX order.
    
      - > Create new vendor order PO for Dropship.

**2.4.6 Step 6 —** **Set Vendor Order Status**

  - > Mark vendor order as Submitted to Vendor.

**2.4.7 Step 7 —** **Print & Attach PO**

  - > Use Print PO option in vendor order.

  - > Save PO as PDF.

  - > Attach the PO PDF to the vendor order in system.

**2.4.8 Step 8 —** **Prepare Vendor Email**

  - > Go to purchasing mailbox.

  - > Open vendor quote email trail.

  - > Click Reply on the same thread.

  - > Use draft:  
    > Please convert this quote into an order.  
    > Vendor PO\# (DKH\_\_\_\_\_\_\_)  
    >   
    > Please change the Shipping address to:  
    > (Customer Name)  
    > (Customer Company if applicable)  
    > (Street Address)  
    > (City, State, ZIP)  
    > United States  
    >   
    > PLEASE SHIP USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \# 632647611 (Only mention if order value is less than $1500)  
    > PLEASE BLIND SHIP**  
    > **

  - > Attach vendor PO PDF.

**2.4.9 Step 9 —Set Recipients**

  - > **TO: Orderentry@orsnasco.com**

  - > **CC:** respective sales rep, Toshiba, and any internal stakeholders per rule.

**2.4.10 Step 10 —Send Email & Update Notes**

  - > Send email to vendor.

  - > In VO notes, mention:
    
      - > Date email sent
    
      - > Email subject line
    
      - > Any special conditions.

**Always Avoid:  
**Sending PO without attaching PDF or without correct shipping address / blind-ship instructions.
