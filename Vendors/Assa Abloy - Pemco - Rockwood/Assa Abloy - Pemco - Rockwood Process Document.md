# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

  - > Minimum Order Value is $2,000.

  - > Orders are placed through the vendor’s website, with the default fulfillment method set to In-House Ground shipping.

  - > Dropshipping is treated as an exception and must follow strict internal rules.

  - > An ETA is never entered for any order type.

  - > Items may be ordered even when stock availability is not displayed on the vendor’s website.

<!-- end list -->

  - > Stock visibility is partial, meaning some items show available stock while others do not.

  - > Backorders are allowed for this vendor.

  - > There are no restricted items.

  - > Discontinued items are clearly displayed in red on the vendor’s website.

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Entering ETA in any field  
❌ Assuming dropship without checking rules  
❌ Assuming stock visibility = availability guarantee

**1.2 Item Validation Rules**

**1.2.1 Discontinued Items:**

  - > Displayed in red text

  - > Action Required:
    
      - > Inform customer immediately
    
      - > Offer alternative or similar items

  - > Do NOT place order

### **2.2 Manual Configuration Items**

Some ASSA ABLOY items require manual configuration. Process:

  - > Enter item number in vendor search

  - > If “Configure” option appears:
    
      - > Configure to match DK Hardware website specs

<!-- end list -->

  - > Add configured item to cart

  - > Minor price differences are acceptable

  - > If unsure → confirm with Elina before ordering

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Adding unconfigured items when configuration is required  
❌ Guessing configurations

## **1.3 Shipping & Fulfillment Rules**

### **1.3.1 In-House Fulfillment (Default)**

  - > Used for all standard orders

  - > Applies regardless of stock visibility

  - > Backorders allowed

  - > Shipping method: Ground

  - > Ship-to: DK Hardware address

### **1.3.2 Dropship Rules (Exception-Only)**

#### **A. Oversized Dropship — Allowed ONLY if ALL are true**

  - > Item is oversized

  - > Item value \> $2,000

**Result:**

  - > Vendor ships directly to customer

  - > Free shipping

  - > No ETA entered

#### **B. Expedited Dropship — Strict Conditions**

Allowed **ONLY if ALL are true**:

  - > Customer explicitly requests expedited shipping

  - > Item shows stock on ASSA ABLOY website

**1.3.3** **Mandatory Customer Disclosure**

  - > Vendor processing time ≈ 1 week

  - > Shipping speed applies after processing

  - > Ask customer:
    
      - > Proceed with expedited **or**
    
      - > Switch to standard ground

⚠️ **If stock is NOT shown → DO NOT DROPSHIP**

## **1.5 Pricing Rules (Universal)**

**Price Adjustment Rule (All Vendors):**

  - > If vendor website price differs slightly:
    
      - > Adjust order price to match vendor website

  - > Applies to ASSA ABLOY and all vendors

✅ Minor price differences are acceptable  
❌ Do not escalate minor variances

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Vendor**

  - > Confirm items belong to ASSA ABLOY

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Check Discontinued**

  - > Red text → stop → inform customer

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Check Configuration**

  - > Configure if required

  - > Confirm with Elina if unsure

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Verify Pricing**

  - > Match vendor website

**2.1.5 Step 5 — Add to Cart**

  - > Add all SKUs

**2.1.6 Step 6** **— Stock Check**

  - > Stock shown → proceed

  - > Stock not shown → proceed (backorders allowed)

**2.1.7 Step 7** **— Checkout**

  - > Shipping: Ground

  - > Address: DK Hardware

**2.1.8 Step 8** **— Payment**

  - > Enter PO number

  - > Use default payment method

**2.1.9 Step 9** **— Final Review**

  - > Quantities, pricing, address

  - > Confirm **NO ETA**

**2.1.10 Step 10** **— Place Order**

  - > Copy confirmation \#

  - > Paste into vendor order record

# **2.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — OVERSIZED DROPSHIP**

2.2.1 Step 1 — Confirm oversized + value \> $2,000  
  
2.2.2 Step 2 — Enter customer address  
  
2.2.3 Step 3 — Confirm free shipping

2.2.4 Step 4 — Do NOT enter ETA  
  
2.2.5 Step 5 — Place order

2.2.6 Step 6 — Record confirmation \#

**2.3 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — EXPEDITED DROPSHIP (Stock Required)**

2.3.1 Step 1 — Confirm customer requested expedited  
  
2.3.2 Step 2 — Confirm item shows stock  
  
2.3.3 Step 3 — Inform customer of \~1 week processing

2.3.4 Step 4 — Get customer confirmation  
  
2.3.5 Step 5 — Enter customer address

2.3.6 Step 6 — Select:

  - > 1-Day **or**

  - > 2-Day shipping

2.3.7 Step 7 — Add note:  
  
*“PLEASE SHIP USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611”  
*  
2.3.8 Step 8 — Place order  
  
2.2.9 Step 9 — Record confirmation \#
