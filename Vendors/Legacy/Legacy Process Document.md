# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Legacy Manufacturing vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

  - > All orders are Dropship ONLY.

  - > Pricing must be confirmed before ordering — never assume.

  - > Every order is considered OPEN until both are received:
    
      - > ✅ Order Acknowledgment (OA)
    
      - > ✅ Tracking Number
    
      - > Audit Rule: OA + Tracking = Closed. Anything else = Open

  - > Active monitoring is mandatory — passive waiting is not allowed.

**ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Sending orders without price confirmation  
❌ Assuming length quantities when UM = Foot (always confirm with customer)  
❌ Shipping freight without Marina approval  
❌ Mentioning the FedEx account on POs  
❌ Closing orders before receiving OA and tracking  
❌ Sending Canada orders without commercial invoice & PO  
❌ Sending Canada orders from Back Office (must use Purchasing email)  
❌ Processing custom orders without signed customer approval  
❌ Providing BOL before the order is confirmed ready

### **1.2 Unit of Measure (UM) Rule**

  - > Most Legacy items (thresholds, sweeps, extrusions) are sold **by length**.

  - > If **UM = Foot**, confirm requirements with the customer before ordering.

  - > **Example (See Figures 1 and 2):  
    > →** Qty = 3

  - > Ask:  
    > → “Do you need one 3-foot piece or three pieces of 1-foot each?”

⚠️ Never assume.

| OA missing        | Order open        | —                   | OA                    | Follow up             |
| Tracking missing  | Order open        | —                   | Tracking              | Follow up             |
