# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > HornerXpress vendor orders are processed directly on the vendor website.

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements for this vendor is $100.

  - > Customer orders do not have any MOV requirement.

  - > Fulfillment Type for all orders is In-House Only.

  - > Dropship orders are not allowed.

  - > Expedited orders are allowed only if the item is in local stock. Otherwise, inform customer of ground-only shipping.

  - > No backorders or discontinued item rules apply.

### **1.2 Stock Verification**

Each item shows one of two stock statuses on the vendor website:

  - > **In Stock (green)** → Proceed normally.

  - > **Available Nearby (orange)** →
    
      - > Check with **Elina** for cost to source from other warehouses.
    
      - > Inform the customer about the **total shipping cost** from our warehouse to their location.
