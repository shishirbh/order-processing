# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > FHC orders are processed via the vendor website.

  - > FHC Minimum Order Value (MOV) can be divided as follows:  
    > → Orders below $2,000 = must be processed In-House  
    > → Orders above $2,000 = may be Dropshipped

## **1.2 Order Fulfillment Logic**

## FHC items are handled by category:

  - ## **Normal (substitutable) items** → typically in-house

  - ## **FHC-only branded items** → must be ordered from FHC

  - ## **Extrusions** → special warehouse & freight rules apply

## **1.3 Warehouses**

FHC warehouses include:

  - > SC

  - > Main (LA)

  - > IRV

  - > WA

Warehouse selection depends on (as discussed in the next sections):

  - > availability

  - > item size

  - > freight rules

  - > backorder capability

## **1.4 Normal (Substitutable) Items**

These items may be sourced from multiple vendors.

**Processing Rules:**

  - > Orders are normally processed In-House.

  - > Assign items to the appropriate available FHC warehouse:

<!-- end list -->

  - > SC

  - > Main (LA)

  - > IRV

  - > WA

  - > NJ (newly added as of Feb 2026)

<!-- end list -->

  - > If the CX order total exceeds $2,000, the order may be Dropshipped.

## **1.5 Non-Substituting (FHC-Only) Items**

Bathroom hardware and branded items must **never be sourced from other vendors**.

### **Includes:**

  - > Shower door hinges

  - > Clamps & brackets

  - > Pull handles

  - > Towel bars

  - > Knbs & grab bars

  - > Sliding shower door systems

  - > Accessories (robe hooks, tissue holders, shower rods, handles)

  - > Any item with FHC/vendor branding

**Fulfillment Rule:**

Below $2,000 → **In-House  
**Above $2,000 → **Dropship**

### **1.6 Items With Minimum Order Quantity**

If an item has a minimum order requirement, quantity must be **rounded up to the next multiple**.

  - > These orders are always processed **In-House**.

  - > Adjust quantity before adding to the warehouse order.

**Example:  
**Customer orders 15 → minimum 10 → order 20.

**1.7 Extrusion Items**

Extrusions may be substituted from other vendors, but if ordering from FHC, warehouse and freight rules apply.

### **1.7.1 Warehouse Rules:**

  - > Items up to **144″** → may be ordered from any FHC warehouse

  - > Items **over 144″** → must be ordered from **SC**

  - > Orders for items longer than 144″ placed from locations other than SC **must include cutting instructions at the time of submission**.

  - > Minimum order rules still apply.

**1.7.2 Shipping Length Rules:**

**→ This rule is applicable for processing Customer Orders ONLY**

Table 1

| **Length**        | **Shipping Method**   |
| ----------------- | --------------------- |
| Up to 95″         | Ground                |
| 95.01″–107″       | Ground + extra charge |
| Above 107″        | Freight               |
| Freight threshold | 105″\+                |

If freight applies **OR** shipment is to:

  - > NYC

  - > Long Island

  - > California

  - > Mountain states

➡ **Marina or Justin must confirm shipping**

### **1.8 Backorder & Availability Rules**

  - > Items marked **“Chat for Availability”** → move to **Main (LA) or SC**

  - > Only LA and SC allow backorders

  - > Stock cannot be transferred from LA to SC. The order will ship directly from SC after the item is received into stock.

  - > IRV & WA ship available items and cancel the rest

  - > Items over 144″ must be placed in **SC**

## **1.9 Vendor Orders (ABOVE $2,000)**

Vendor orders are processed only when order value exceeds $2,000.

### **1.9.1 Warehouse Processing Order:**

  - > IRV / WA / SC / NJ processed first

  - > Main (LA) processed last

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING NORMAL (SUBSTITUTABLE) & FHC ONLY ITEMS — IN-HOUSE**

**Steps:**

2.1.1 Step 1 — Open the customer order.

2.1.2 Step 2 — Perform fraud check.

2.1.3 Step 3 — Click DK hyperlink → Vendor hyperlink.

2.1.4 Step 4 — Verify item code, description, and price.

2.1.5 Step 5 — Check FHC website:

  - > confirm stock

  - > confirm warehouse location

2.1.6 Step 6 — If details match, click ‘Processing’

2.1.7 Step 7 — Assign item to the correct FHC warehouse as In-House.

### **2.2 PROCESSING EXTRUSION ITEMS — IN-HOUSE**

**Steps:**

2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify extrusion size

2.2.2 Step 2 — Determine freight applicability.

2.2.3 Step 3 — Confirm stock on FHC website.

2.2.4 Step 4 — Assign to correct warehouse.

2.2.5 Step 5 — Process as In-House.

### **2.3 PROCESSING DROPSHIP ORDER — 1 & 2 BUSINESS DAY** 

### **Eligibility Requirements:**

  - > Cannot exceed 95 inches (cutting information is necessary)

  - > Must be in stock

  - > Must be Dropshipped

**Steps:**

2.3.1 Step 1 — Validate customer order & item.

2.3.2 Step 2 — Create dropship vendor order.

2.3.3 Step 3 — Log in to FHC website.

2.3.4 Step 4 — Add items to cart.

2.3.5 Step 5 — Select UPS Next Day or 2nd Day Air.

2.3.6 Step 6 — Enter customer shipping address.

2.3.7 Step 7 — Place order.

### **2.4 PROCESSING FHC SPECIAL ORDERS (QUOTE-BASED)**

**→ All special orders are processed via the FHC website (not email).**

**Quote Verification Steps:**

2.4.1 Step 1 — Log in to FHC website.

2.4.2 Step 2 — Click Quotes.

2.4.3 Step 3 — Search quote number.

2.4.4 Step 4 — Open and verify:

  - > item codes

  - > descriptions

  - > sizes

  - > quantities

  - > pricing

  - > customer details

**All details must match:**

  - > vendor quote

  - > signed customer quote

  - > vendor order in Back Office

→ If anything does not match → DO NOT proceed.

**Submitting Quote Order Steps:**

2.4.5 Step 5 — Enter PO number in quote checkout.

2.4.6 Step 6 — Attach Vendor Order in Back Office.

2.4.7 Step 7 — In Order Comments, include instructions for Tina:

  - > warehouse

  - > freight

  - > special handling

2.4.8 Step 8 — Place order through website.
