  - > Not available from alternate vendors

**1.6 Freight & Oversized Item Rules**

  - > Always check freight charges with Marina before processing.

  - > Do not order freight items from other warehouses.

  - > Items over 96 inches must not be ordered from alternate warehouses.

**1.7 Special Item Programs & Multiple Carts**

Some special programs generate **separate carts**.

Before submission:

  - > Add totals from all carts.

  - > Ensure combined total matches the Vendor Order.

### **1.8 ETA** 

  - > Orders processed **Tuesday–Thursday** → Delivered **Monday**

  - > Orders processed **Friday–Monday** → Delivered **Wednesday**

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PRE-PROCESSING CHECKS - Received Partials Review (Mandatory First Step)**

# → Received partials must be reviewed for both main and other warehouse orders.

**STEPS:**

2.1.1 Step 1 — Click View & Edit  
2.1.2 Step 2 — Select Purchase Orders  
2.1.3 Step 3 — Apply filter → Status → Received Partial  
2.1.4 Step 4 — Review orders

**Action Rules:  
**If invoice notes show:

  - > **Discontinued  
    > ** → No further action required  
    > → Inform customer

  - > **Temporarily Out of Stock – Please Reorder**  
    > → Move item to warehouse where stock is available  
    > → Mark as Received Complete

# **2.2 INVENTORY ANd WAREHOUSE MOVEMENT RULES**

If item is not available in Tifton, GA:

2.2.1 Step 1 — Check other Orgill warehouses.  
2.2.2 Step 2 — Move item to warehouse with stock.  
2.2.3 Step 3 — If unavailable everywhere → check alternate vendors.  
2.2.4 Step 4 — If still unavailable → move to Out-of-Stock Order.

⚠️ Do not order freight items or items over 96 inches from other warehouses.

**2.3 CART AND PRICING VALIDATION**

**STEPS:**

2.3.1 Step 1 — Verify pricing while adding items.  
2.3.2 Step 2 — Cart total may not exactly match Vendor Order total.  
2.3.3 Step 3 — Orgill will display restriction notifications.

### **→ Restricted Items:**

  - > Cannot be ordered.

  - > Move to alternate vendor or inform customer.

# **2.4 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE  
  
STEPS:  
  
**2.4.1 Step 1 — Confirm item details and totals.  
2.4.2 Step 2 — Click Create Order.  
2.4.3 Step 3 — Enter the PO Number.  
2.4.4 Step 4 — Click Purchase All.  
2.4.5 Step 5 — Copy the Order Confirmation Number.  
2.4.6 Step 6 — Paste confirmation number into Vendor Order (VO).
