# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > All orders are processed via Backoffice email.

  - > Vendor Email: [<span class="underline">aalbright@shureusa.com</span>](mailto:aalbright@shureusa.com)

  - > All ShureSAFE orders are processed as dropship orders.

  - > There are NO special-order items requiring vendor quotes or customer signatures.

  - > There is no minimum order quantity.

  - > Monitor inbox after submission for:  
    > → confirmations  
    > → change notices  
    > → shipping updates

  - > Respond promptly to avoid delays.

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Sending orders without using Backoffice email workflow  
❌ Processing large items without shipping approval  
❌ Using expedited shipping without UPS Collect instructions  
❌ Processing Canada orders without approval  
❌ Never place orders via vendor portals  
❌ Never call the vendor unless explicitly instructed by senior staff

### **1.2 Mandatory Order Classification**

Before processing, confirm:

**Destination:**

  - > United States

  - > Canada

**Shipping Type:**

  - > Standard parcel

  - > Large / heavy / oversized

**Shipping Speed:**

  - > Standard

  - > 1 Business Day

  - > 2 Business Day

→ No processing should begin until classification is complete.

**1.4 Canada Orders**

→ ShureSAFE Canada orders are not processed automatically.

**If a Canada order appears:**

  - > Do not process immediately.

  - > Check with Yana or Kirill for approval.

  - > Proceed only after confirmation**.**

**Important for Canada Dropship Orders:**

  - > Whenever possible, request shipment via the vendor’s UPS account. This allows the order to ship without requiring our commercial invoice and can be processed using the standard dropship email draft.

  - > For large quantities or high-value shipments, the vendor may request broker details.

  - > To avoid delays, proactively confirm brokerage information with Elina or Kirill before submitting the order when shipment size or value may trigger customs requirements.

# **3.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### → Default method for all orders.

  - ### Create the vendor order in Backoffice.

  - ### Send order using UPS Collect email template.

### UPS account ensures:

  - ### shipping billed to us

  - ### no vendor delays

# **3.3 PROCESSING A DROPSHIP ORDER - LARGE / FREIGHT ITEMS**

### **3.3.1 Step 1 — Identify Large Item**

  - ### Confirm if item is oversized or heavy.

### **3.3.2 Step 2 — Request Dimensions**

  - ### If needed, email vendor requesting shipping dimensions.

### **3.3.3 Step 3 — Share with Marina**

  - ### Provide dimensions, weight, and ship-from location.

### **3.3.4 Step 4 — Approval Decision**

  - ### ✔ If approved → proceed

  - ### ❌ If not approved → inform customer

### **3.3.5 Step 5 — Submit Order**

  - > Send dropship order after approval.
