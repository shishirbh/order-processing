# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

  - > No Minimum Order Value - Vendor accepts orders of any amount.

  - > Order Method is via Email in the BackOffice only

  - > Order Submission Frequency (for In-House orders) : Once weekly — Every Friday only

  - > Standard ETA: 7 calendar days (unless explicitly changed by vendor)

  - > Dropship allowed only under specific circumstances as discussed in section 1.2

⚠️ KEY VENDOR POINT:  
Drill America orders are batched and submitted weekly. Do **not** submit orders outside Friday unless explicitly approved by a senior.

**1.2 Dropship Rules & Eligibility**

**Dropship is NOT default.** Dropship is allowed only when one or more of the conditions below are met.

#### **1.2.1 Eligible for Dropship:**

Dropship only if:

  - > Customer requires delivery in 1–2 business days, OR

  - > Item is lengthy or oversized and must ship directly from vendor

#### **1.2.2 Mandatory Dropship Rules:**

  - > All dropship orders MUST ship using the DK FedEx account

  - > Dropship must be explicitly mentioned in the vendor email

  - > Dropship applies per order, not per item (unless approved)

🚫 **Always Avoid**

  - > Dropshipping for convenience

  - > Dropshipping without time urgency or size justification

  - > Using any shipping account other than DK FedEx.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 INTERNAL PROCESSING STEPS (for In-House and Dropship Orders)**

**2.1.1 Step 1 —** **Validate Item History (Critical Check)**

  - > Do NOT check vendor website

  - > Validate item only through customer’s past order history

  - > Confirm:
    
      - > Was the item previously cancelled?
    
      - > Were there pricing, stock, or fulfillment issues?
    
      - > Any discrepancies or notes from prior orders?

⚠️ **If issues are found:  
**Pause processing → Flag internally → Proceed only after confirmation.

**2.1.2 Step 2 —** **Clean Vendor Notes**

  - > Remove:
    
      - > Outdated notes
    
      - > Internal comments
    
      - > Irrelevant past references

  - > Vendor notes must contain only current, order-relevant information

🚫 **Always Avoid**

  - > Leaving internal comments visible to vendor

  - > Copying notes from previous POs without review

**2.1.3 Step 3 —** **Submit Order via Email (Friday Only)**

**Email Template (Strict Format):**

Hi Team,

Kindly process the attached order.

Please let us know if there are any changes.

Thank you.

**Attachments & Mentions**

  - > Attach Purchase Order (PO)

  - > **Clearly mention “Dropship Required” if applicable**

  - > No additional commentary unless necessary

**2.1.4 Step 4 —** **Review Vendor Response (Mandatory)**

Monitor vendor reply for:

  - > Price changes

  - > Stock availability issues

  - > Corrections requested

  - > Shipping exceptions

**If ANY change is mentioned:**

  - > Update the order internally

  - > Revise the PO

  - > Resend the corrected PO via email

⚠️ **Never assume silence = acceptance  
** Always confirm vendor acknowledgment.

**2.1.5 Step 5 — ETA Handling**

  - > Standard ETA: 7 days

  - > Applies to all orders unless vendor explicitly communicates otherwise

  - > Any deviation must be:
    
      - > Documented
    
      - > Communicated to the customer (if applicable)
