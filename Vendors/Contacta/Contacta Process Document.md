# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 MANDATORY FIRST STEP - Orders Classification**

Before processing, confirm:

**Destination:**

  - > Domestic (US)

  - > International

**Shipping Type:**

  - > Standard parcel

  - > Large / bulk / oversized

⚠️ No processing should begin until classification is complete.

### **1.2 Vendor Rules**

  - > Contacta vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

  - > No Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements for this vendor.

  - > All orders are processed as Dropship Orders, regardless of:  
    > → Tax status  
    > → Destination state  
    > → Domestic vs. international shipment

  - > Monitor inbox after submission for:  
    > → confirmations  
    > → change notices  
    > → shipping updates

  - > Respond promptly to avoid delays.

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Sending dropship orders without FedEx account details  
❌ Processing large items without approval  
❌ Sending international orders without confirmation  
❌ Allowing shipment before BOL is issued (freight cases)

### **1.3 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

### **1.3.1 Standard Items:**

  - > All **standard-sized items** → **Automatically eligible**

  - > No approval required

### **1.3.2 Large / Bulk / Oversized Items:**

→ These **DO NOT auto-qualify for dropship**.

**→ Mandatory Approval Process (Before Any Submission):**

  - > Consult **Elina**

  - > Obtain **shipping dimensions**

  - > Share dimensions with **Marina** to validate shipping cost

**→ Cost Evaluation Origin Address (Must be used):**

4025 Tampa Road  
Suite 1107A  
Tampa, FL 34677

#### **→ Decision Outcome:**

  - > **Approved** → Proceed with dropship

  - > **Not approved** → Inform customer (do not submit order)

⚠️ **Never send large items without approval.** This is a cost-risk control.

## **1.3.3 International Orders**

### **Rules:**

  - > All international orders are **dropshipped**

  - > Mandatory confirmation with Yana **BEFORE** order submission

### **What to Confirm:**

  - > Shipping feasibility

  - > Compliance readiness

  - > Cost acceptance

⚠️ **No email to vendor until Yana confirms.**

### **1.4 FedEx Account Rule (CRITICAL CONTROL)**

### **Applies To:**

  - > ALL standard dropship orders

### **Rule:**

  - > FedEx Account MUST be included

  - > Account Number: **632647611**

❌ **Dropship orders MUST NOT be sent without the FedEx account mentioned.** This is a hard stop.

**Used For:**

## **2.2 Special Dropship Scenario — BOL REQUIRED**

### **When This Applies:**

  - > Large / oversized item is approved

  - > Freight arranged by us

  - > A **Bill of Lading (BOL)** will be issued by us

**EMAIL:**

To: orders@contactainc.com

Hi,

Please ship this order directly to our customer.

Please let us know once the order is ready.

We will provide you with a bill of lading.

Kindly let us know if any changes.

Regards,  
Purchasing Team

### **Critical Rules:**

❌ **DO NOT include FedEx account  
**❌ **Shipment must NOT move until:**

  - > Order-ready confirmation received

  - > BOL is issued and shared

⚠️ Using the wrong template here causes freight billing failures.

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — STANDARD DROPSHIP**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Create Vendor Order**

  - ### Create the vendor order in Backoffice.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Verify Customer Address**

  - ### Confirm shipping address accuracy (mandatory).

### **3.1.3 Step 3 —** Submit by Email

  - ### Send order to vendor using:

  - ### Backoffice email

  - ### Standard Dropship Template

### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Include FedEx Account**

  - ### Ensure FedEx account \#632647611 is included.

**3.1.5 Step 5 — Monitor Vendor Response**

  - > Watch for confirmation or updates.

# **3.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — LARGE ITEM DROPSHIP (BOL REQUIRED)**

**3.1.1 Step 1 — Request Dimensions**

  - > Obtain shipping dimensions from vendor.

**3.2.2 Step 2 — Approval Process**

  - > Share dimensions with Marina for shipping cost approval.

**3.2.3 Step 3 — Decision**

✔ If approved → proceed

❌ If not → inform customer

**3.2.4 Step 4 — Submit Order**

  - > Send using BOL email template.

**3.2.5 Step 5 — Issue BOL**

  - > Provide Bill of Lading once order is ready.

# **3.3 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — INTERNATIONAL DROPSHIP**

  - > Obtain approval before submission.

**3.3.2 Step 2 — Verify Address**

  - > Confirm international shipping details.

**3.3.3 Step 3 — Submit Order**

  - > Send using Standard Dropship Template.
