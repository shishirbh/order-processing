### **1.1 Key Rules & Notes**

  - > US Horizon orders can be processed as:

<!-- end list -->

  - > In-House Orders

  - > Dropship Orders

<!-- end list -->

  - > Processing method depends on:

<!-- end list -->

  - > order value

  - > shipping feasibility

  - > item type

**1.2 Minimum Order Requirement**

Table 1

| **Condition**       | **Rule**                                 |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Order value ≥ $100  | Meets vendor minimum                     |
| Order value \< $100 | Order allowed but shipping charges apply |

Orders below the minimum value may still be processed but require **shipping cost consideration**.

**1.3 Dropship Rules**

→ Dropshipping is allowed for most items, except freight items.

  - ### **Dropship Allowed:**

> ✔ Standard items  
> ✔ Orders below minimum (shipping cost must be evaluated)  
> ✔ Items requiring cutting instructions

  - ### Dropship Not Allowed

> ❌ Freight items

**1.4 Stock Verification**

Before placing any order:

  - > Verify stock availability on the US Horizon website.

→ This step is mandatory to avoid order delays.

**1.5 Processing Frequency & ETA**

**1.5.1 Processing Frequency:**

  - > In-house orders should be processed once per day.

**1.5.2 Recommended approach:**

  - > Combine orders during the day

  - > Submit vendor order once daily

  - > Preferably process the day before Going

→ This helps reduce multiple vendor submissions.

### **1.5.3 Estimated Delivery Time:**

  - > Incoming orders typically have an ETA of approximately 1 week.

**1.6 Shipping Method Handling**

For expedited orders:

Table 2

| **Shipping Speed**        | **Action**                            |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Standard delivery         | Select Ground shipping                |
| 1–2 business day delivery | Verify shipping cost first            |
| Ground not listed         | Select “Other” and specify UPS Ground |

**1.7 Cutting Instruction for Extrusion (IMPORTANT)**

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE** 

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Product**

Check the US Horizon website for:

  - > item availability

  - > SKU accuracy

  - > product description

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Add Items to Cart**

Add the required items to the vendor cart and verify:

  - > quantity

  - > item specifications

**2.1.3 Step 3 — Select Shipping Type**

  - > Choose:

**→ Ship and Backorder**

  - > This allows the vendor to automatically place out-of-stock items on **backorder**.

### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Enter Purchase Order**

  - > Enter the **Purchase Order (PO) number**.

### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Select Payment Method**

  - ### Choose:

### **→ Saved Cards**

  - ### Select the appropriate saved card.

### **2.1.6 Step 6 — Place Order**

  - > Submit the order on the vendor website.

# **2.2 PROCESSING CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify Product**

Check the US Horizon website for:

  - > item availability

  - > SKU accuracy

  - > product description

  - > Include cutting instructions if required

**2.2.2 Step 2 — Review Shipping Options**

For expedited shipments:

  - > check **1–2 business day shipping options**

  - > verify shipping cost

**2.2.3 Step 3 — Shipping Decision**

Based on shipping cost:

  - > Inform the customer of the shipping charges, or

  - > Proceed with dropshipping.

**2.2.4 Step 4 — Add Item to Cart**

  - > Add the item to the vendor cart.

**2.2.5 Step 5 — Enter Customer Shipping Address**

  - > Enter the **customer shipping details**.

  - > Verify accuracy before proceed

**2.2.6 Step 6 — Select Shipping Method**

  - > Choose the appropriate shipping method.

  - > Default option:

→ **Ground Shipping**

  - > If Ground shipping does not appear:

→ Select **Other** and add:

**→ UPS Ground** in the shipping method notes.

**2.2.7 Step 7 — Place Order**

  - > Complete the order through the vendor website.

**2.2.8 Step 8 — Place Order**

  - > Click **Place Order** on the vendor website.

**2.2.9 Step 9 — Record Confirmation**

  - > Copy the Order Confirmation Number.

  - > Add confirmation details to Vendor Order Notes

**SECTION 3 — DECISION MATRIX**

Table 3 - US Horizon Processing Decision Matrix

| **Scenario**                | **Action**               | **Notes**                 |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------- |
| Order value ≥ $100          | Process normally         | Meets minimum order value |
| Order value \< $100         | Process order            | Shipping charges apply    |
| Standard items              | Dropship allowed         | Direct to customer        |
| Freight items               | In-House only            | Dropship not allowed      |
| Item out of stock           | Use “Ship and Backorder” | Vendor will backorder     |
| Expedited order (1–2 days)  | Verify shipping cost     | Inform customer if needed |
| Ground shipping unavailable | Select “Other”           | Specify UPS Ground        |
| Extrusions / channels       | Add cutting instructions | Vendor performs cutting   |
