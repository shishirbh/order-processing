# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Overview**

  - > EJ Main Warehouse orders are processed via the vendor portal.

  - > MOV is $1000 per week

  - > Primary Warehouse:

> → FL02 (Plant City, FL) — primary warehouse for DK Hardware.

  - > Always prefer FL02 for:

> → Freight items  
> → Long items (up to 96")  
> → All in-house orders when available  
> → Dropship from FL02 is allowed (Updated: 2026-04-26)

  - > Other EJ warehouses are used only when FL02 is out of stock.

  - > Dropship is allowed ONLY from other (non-FL02) warehouses.

  - > Dropship is supported only under conditions outlined in Section 1.2.

  - > EJ does not provide quotes. No SO, freight, customization, or special-order quotes required.

  - > In-house ETA: approximately 7 days

  - > Always copy vendor confirmation number.

  - > Never mix dropship and in-house items in one vendor order.

**Always Avoid:**

✔ Dropship from FL02 is allowed (Updated: 2026-04-26)  
❌ Do NOT process ESCPO items  
❌ Do NOT request quotes from EJ  
❌ Do NOT ignore seasonal/OFS notes  
❌ Do NOT close VO before full receiving  
❌ Do NOT skip alternate vendor checks  
❌ Do NOT proceed if totals don’t match

## **1.2 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

**1.2.1 Warehouse & Weight Logic**

Table 1

| **Condition**                                   | **Action**                                |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Item available in FL02                          | In-House                                  |
| Not in FL02 but available in other EJ warehouse | Dropship (\>1 lb) / In-House (\<1 lb)     |
| Item \< 1 lb                                    | May process In-House from other warehouse |
| Item \> 1 lb                                    | Dropship if not in FL02                   |
| Freight or long item                            | Prefer FL02; if unavailable → Dropship    |
| Not available in any EJ warehouse               | Check alternate vendors                   |
| Canada / International order                    | Dropship (from non-FL02 warehouse)        |

### **1.2.2 Dropship is ALLOWED when:**

  - ## Item is not available in FL02

  - ## Item weighs more than 1 lb

  - ## Freight or long item unavailable in FL02

  - ## International / Canada order

  - ## Dropship is operationally required

## **1.3 Handling OFS, Seasonal & Discontinued Items**

EJ does not use special order quotes.

If EJ shows:

  - > **OFS** → Check alternate vendors

  - > **Seasonal + OFS** → Mark Discontinued

  - > **Cancelled** → Mark Discontinued

  - > **ESCPO items** → DO NOT process

⚠️ Never process discontinued items further

## **1.4 Alternate Vendor Use (Customer Orders Only)**

→ Triggered **only when EJ is OFS/OOS**.

### **Vendor Check Order:**

  - > TrueValue

  - > Orgill (ONLY if Tifton main warehouse)

  - > Do It Best

### **Selection Rule:**

  - > Choose **cheapest + fastest**

  - > If all vendors OFS → move to **RSCPO (Awaiting Restock)**

❌ EJ orders must **never** be held waiting for quotes.

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**Eligibility:**

  - > Item available in FL02

  - > Item \<1 lb (any warehouse)

  - > Dropship not operationally required

**Steps:**

**2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Customer**

  - > Verify customer details via Google / White Pages.

  - > Confirm phone, email, billing & shipping.

  - > Check credit card match.

  - > If mismatch → hold for verification.

**2.1.2 Step 2 — Verify Item**

**Verify on:**

  - > DK Hardware website

  - > EJ Vendor Portal

**Match:**

  - > Image

  - > Description

  - > Specs

  - > Weight

  - > Availability

  - > Price.

**2.1.3 Step 3 — OFS / Discontinued Rules**

**If:**

  - > OFS → check alternate vendors

  - > Seasonal + OFS → discontinued

  - > Cancelled → discontinued

**2.1.4 Step 4 — Assign Products**

✔ Assign local stock first.  
✔ If local stock unavailable → assign entire quantity from EJ.

**Status Logic:**

  - > Full EJ assignment → Coming In-House

  - > Partial local + EJ → Coming In-House with Backorder

  - > Red → Green after assignment

**2.1.5 Step 5 —** **Vendor Order Selection**

  - > Choose Use Existing Vendor Order if open.

  - > Otherwise → Create New Order → Select In-House.

**2.1.6 Step 6** **— Payment**

**Authorize & charge except:**

  - > Amazon

  - > Zoro

  - > Net 30

**Order status:**

  - > New → Coming In-House

**2.1.7 Step 7** **— Process Changes**

**System updates:**

  - > Status

  - > Assigned quantities

  - > Vendor order linkage

### **2.2 PROCESSING A CUSTOMER ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **Eligibility:**

  - > Item not in FL02

  - > Item \> 1 lb

  - > Freight/long item not in FL02

  - > Canada / international customer

⚠️ EJ never dropships from FL02.

**Steps:**

**2.2.1 Step 1 — Verify Customer**

  - > Verify customer details via Google / White Pages.

  - > Confirm phone, email, billing & shipping.

  - > Check credit card match.

  - > If mismatch → hold for verification.

**2.2.2 Step 2 — Verify Item**

**Verify on:**

  - > DK Hardware website

  - > EJ Vendor Portal

**Match:**

  - > Image

  - > Description

  - > Specs

  - > Weight

  - > Availability

  - > Price.

**2.2.3 Step 3 — Assign Products**

→ Assign only from EJ Main warehouse.

**Status:  
**Placed for Dropship

**2.2.4 Step 4 — Create Vendor Order**

  - > Create New Order → Select Dropship.

**2.2.5 Step 5 — Payment**

**Charge except:**

  - > Amazon

  - > Zoro

  - > Net 30

**Order status:**

New → Placed for Dropship

**2.2.6 Step 6 — Finalize**

  - > Click Process Changes.

  - > VO number generated.

### **2.3 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

**Steps:**

**2.3.1 Step 1 — Pre-Check**

  - > Confirm FL02 availability

  - > Validate items & pricing

**2.3.2 Step 2 — Build Order**

  - > Add items one by one

**Validate:**

  - > Price

  - > Qty

  - > Description

  - > Replacement alerts

  - > Cancelled SKU alerts

**2.3.3 Step 3 — Cart Match**

  - > Compare EJ cart vs VO totals.

  - > Resolve mismatches before proceeding.

**If mismatch:**

  - > Check missing SKU

  - > Qty error

  - > Price difference

  - > Replacement

  - > OFS → move to new VO

  - > Cancelled → mark discontinued

**2.3.4 Step 4 — Submit Order**

  - > Add PO number

  - > Final total verification

  - > Submit

**2.3.5 Step 5 — Record Confirmation**

  - > Paste EJ Confirmation Number ONLY into VO notes

### **2.4 RECEIVING & INVOICE VERIFICATION**

### **2.4.1 When Shipment Arrives**

  - > Partial shipments possible.

  - > EJ invoices per shipment.

### **2.4.2 Invoice Matching**

Match:

  - > SKU

  - > Qty

  - > Price

  - > Freight (if any)

If mismatch:

  - > Correct BO

  - > OR move unshipped items to new VO

### **2.4.3 Receiving Status**

  - > Shipped & billed → Mark Received

  - > Not shipped & not billed → Move to new VO

  - > Goal: **Receive Complete ASAP**
