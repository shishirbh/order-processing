# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Bluewater Supply vendor orders are processed only by email using Backoffice (BO).

  - > All Blue Water shipments use our shipping account and labels.

  - > Shipping labels must be created by Marina.

  - > Always obtain package weight and dimensions before generating a shipping label.

  - > Vendor ships directly to the customer using the provided label.

  - > Verify shipment size and method before confirming large orders.

**IMPORTANT:**

❌ Do not generate shipping labels without confirmed weight and dimensions.  
❌ Do not process large or bulk orders without confirming shipping feasibility.  
❌ Do not assume shipping method (FedEx vs LTL) without verification.

### **1.2 Freight Policy**

Table 1 - Freight Fulfillment Rules **(Exceptions mentioned below Table 1)**

| **Order Value**        | **Freight Rule**                         |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Under $350             | Freight charges apply                    |
| Over $350              | Free freight via UPS                     |
| LTL orders over $1,000 | Free freight (excluding hazardous items) |

### **Items Excluded from Free Freight** 

**1.2.1 Hazardous materials:**

  - > paint

  - > resin

  - > solvent

  - > Batteries

**1.2.2 Oversized product:**

  - > Drums

  - > boat stands

  - > DIAB

  - > COOSA

  - > CARBON CORE

  - > SINOMA

### **1.3 Repairs & Returns**

Tool repairs or NS items returned to vendor incur freight charges for both:

  - > Shipping to vendor

  - > Return shipping
