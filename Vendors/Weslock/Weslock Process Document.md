# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Weslock vendor orders are processed through emails via Backoffice (BO).

  - > Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirement for this vendor is $250 for all order types.

  - > Dropship allowed for standard ground shipping if of order value is ≥ $250.

  - > Expedited Orders (1–2 Business Day) require Elina’s approval before processing the order. Expedited orders do not need to meet the MOV

  - > Vendor acknowledgment is mandatory before order is considered placed.

**Always Avoid:**

❌ Send expedited orders without approval  
❌ Include internal notes  
❌ Use wrong subject line or template  
❌ Assume acknowledgment  
❌ Email wrong vendor address  
❌ Use outdated customer address

### **1.2 Order Types & How to Process**

#### **1.2.1 In-House Vendor Orders**

  - > **Shipping:** Standard Ground only

  - > **MOV:** $250

  - > **Template:** Standard Processing

  - > **Address:** DK Hardware warehouse

**Example:  
**A $325 restock order shipping to DK warehouse → Standard Template → Submit → Email → Wait for acknowledgment

#### **1.2.2 Customer Orders – Standard Ground**

  - > **Processed same as in-house**

  - > **MOV:** $250

  - > **Template:** Standard Processing

  - > **Ship To:** Customer address

#### **1.2.3 Dropship Orders**

  - > **Eligibility:** Order total **≥ $250**

  - > **Shipping:** Standard Ground

  - > **Template:** Dropship – Standard Ground

  - > **Ship To:** Customer address

**Example:  
**Customer order $410 → Dropship allowed → Dropship template → Email → Wait for acknowledgment

**1.3 Edge Case Handling**

Table 1

| **Scenario**           | **Required Action**                      |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| In-House Order \< $250 | Add items OR escalate to supervisor      |
| Remote area expedited  | Confirm approval again                   |
| Incomplete address     | Contact customer → correct before submit |
| Vendor price change    | Update BO → confirm with customer        |
| Customer wants upgrade | Follow expedited approval flow           |
| No vendor reply        | Follow up after 24 hours                 |

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR AND CUSTOMER ORDER — IN-HOUSE / DROPSHIP**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 — Pre-Submission Verification**

Confirm ALL:

  - > Order total ≥ $250 (except expedited orders)

  - > Correct pricing & quantities

  - > Correct shipping method

  - > Correct address (customer vs warehouse)

### **3.1.2 Step 2 — Move Order to “Submit”**

  - > Only after verification is complete.

### **3.1.3 Step 3 — Email Processing**

  - > Click **“By Email”** in Backoffice

  - > Select correct template:
    
      - > Standard Ground
    
      - > Dropship Standard
    
      - > Next Day (Approved only)
    
      - > Second Day (Approved only)

  - > Confirm subject line matches template

#### **3.1.4 Step 4 — Final Checks (Zero-Error Gate)**

  - > Remove **all internal notes**

  - > Subject line correct

  - > Shipping instructions correct

  - > Correct vendor email selected

### **3.1.5 Step 5 — Vendor Acknowledgment**

  - > **Wait for acknowledgment**

  - > If **no response in 24 hours** → resend using:

**Subject:** FOLLOW UP – Awaiting Acknowledgment
