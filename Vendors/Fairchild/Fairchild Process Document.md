# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 MANDATORY FIRST STEP - Orders Classification**

Every order must be classified before submission. All Classification Factors must be checked):

  - > Destination state tax status

  - > Item size (standard vs large/bulk/oversized)

  - > Shipping method (FedEx vs Freight)

  - > International vs domestic order

  - > Order value

  - > Dropship approval status (if required)

  - > Fairchild Industries vendor orders are processed **only by email** using Backoffice (BO).

## **ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Dropshipping to non-tax-exempt states  
❌ Sending dropship emails without FedEx account  
❌ Assuming approval for large items  
❌ Delaying orders past Friday  
❌ Mixing templates or modifying language  
❌ Mentioning FedEx account in in-house orders above $3,500

### **1.2 IN-HOUSE ORDERS**

**1.2.1 Mandatory In-House Scenarios**

Orders must be processed In-House if any of the following apply:

  - > International orders (always)

  - > Shipping to Non-Tax-Exempt States (see Figure 1)

  - > Dropship was not approved

  - > Shipping cost for dropship is not viable

**1.2.2 The $3,500 Minimum Order Rule**

  - > Target minimum order value**:** $3,500

  - > Purpose: shipping consolidation & cost efficiency

⚠️ This is a **target**, not a blocker.

**1.2.3 Friday Exception Rule (ABSOLUTE)**

If vendor order value does NOT reach $3,500 by Friday:

  - > Order MUST still be processed

  - > Ship using our FedEx account \#632647611

  - > Do NOT delay beyond Friday

  - > No carryover allowed

❌ Delaying past Friday is a compliance violation.

### **1.2.4 FedEx Rule for In-House Orders**

  - > Use general email draft

  - > Do NOT mention FedEx account for orders above $3,500

  - > FedEx account may be used internally, but never stated in email

### **1.3 DROPSHIP ORDERS**

### **1.3.1 Eligibility Criteria (ALL Must Be True)**

Dropship is allowed **only if**:

  - > Shipping destination is a Tax-Exempt State

  - > Items are standard-sized

  - > Items are not large, bulk, or oversized  
    > OR special approval has been granted

❌ If any condition fails, dropship is NOT allowed → classify as In-House

### **1. 3.2 Approval Process for Large / Bulk / Oversized Items**

Dropship for large or bulk items is NOT automatic.

Mandatory Approval Steps (No Shortcuts):

1.  > Consult Toshiba or Elina

2.  > Obtain exact shipping dimensions

3.  > Share dimensions with Marina

4.  > Marina evaluates shipping cost viability

Evaluation Origin Address (Must Be Used):**  
**📍 *475 Capital Drive, Lake Zurich, IL 60047*

#### Approval Outcome:

  - > ✅ Approved → Proceed with dropship

  - > ❌ Not Approved → Inform customer → Process as In-House

⚠️ Never assume approval. Written confirmation is required.

### **1.3.3 FedEx Rule for Dropship Orders (CRITICAL):**

  - > Dropship email must include our FedEx account number

  - > Orders must not be sent without FedEx account reference

**Always Verify:  
**✔ Customer address accuracy  
✔ Tax-exempt status  
✔ Correct email template usage

  - ### Pricing correct

  - ### Quantities accurate

  - ### Shipping method selected

  - ### Meets minimum order value

  - ### No restricted notes

### Then → Move vendor order to SUBMIT.

  - ### In Backoffice:

  - ### Click By Email

  - ### Select correct shipping template

    → Bill of Lading Required
