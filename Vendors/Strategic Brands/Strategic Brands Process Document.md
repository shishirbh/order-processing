# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Strategic Brands vendor orders are processed directly on the vendor website.

  - > There are no Minimum Order Value (MOV) requirements for this vendor.

  - > Dropship orders are placed for heavy, oversized items, and expedited orders.

  - > Dropship is carried out through FedEx Only.

**ALWAYS AVOID:**

❌ Never dropship without entering FedEx account number  
❌ Never skip vendor stock verification  
❌ Never manually re-type customer addresses  
❌ Never dropship items that are not heavy, oversized, or expedited  
❌ Never assume FedEx is allowed for in-house orders

### **1.2 Order Type Classification**

Every Strategic Brands order must be classified before placement.

Table 1 Order Types

| **Order Type** | **Allowed**        | **Notes**                                            |
| -------------- | ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| In-House Order | ✅ Yes              | Default order type                                   |
| Dropship Order | ✅ Yes (restricted) | Only for specific scenarios discussed in Section 1.3 |

**1.3 Dropship Eligibility Rules**

Dropshipping is NOT default. It is allowed ONLY when at least one of the following conditions is true:

### **Dropship-Eligible Conditions:**

  - > Item is heavy

  - > Item is oversized

  - > Customer selected expedited shipping

If none of the above apply → **DO NOT DROPSHIP**

**1.4 Shipping Method Rules**

### **1.4.1 In-House Orders**

  - > Shipping Method: Standard Ground

  - > Carrier: Vendor’s preferred carrier

  - > FedEx Account: ❌ Never used

### **1.4.2 Dropship Orders**

  - > Shipping Method: FedEx only

  - > FedEx Account:
    
      - > Must be entered in order notes during checkout
    
      - > Required for every dropship order

  - > Customer Address:
    
      - > Must be copied & pasted exactly into vendor checkout

## **1.5 Exception Handling & Edge Cases**

### **1.5.1 Out-of-Stock Item**

  - > Notify customer immediately

  - > Provide revised shipping timeline

  - > Do not place order without confirmation in case of delay

### **1.5.2 Incorrect / Incomplete Address**

  - > Stop processing

  - > Contact customer for correction

  - > Resume only after confirmation

### **1.5.3 Dropship Without FedEx Account**

  - > ❌ Order must NOT be placed

  - > Correct before checkout

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

## **2.1 PROCESSING VENDOR AND CUSTOMER ORDER — INHOUSE & DROPSHIP** 

## **2.1.1 Step 1 — Visit Vendor Website**

  - > Navigate to Strategic Brands website

  - > Locate the product page

### **2.1.2 Step 2: Stock Verification (MANDATORY)**

  - > Confirm real-time stock availability on the product page

  - > If **out of stock**:
    
      - > Do **NOT** place order
    
      - > Notify customer
    
      - > Confirm revised ship timeline

### **2.1.3 Step 3: Determine Fulfillment Type**

  - > Ask:
    
      - > Is item heavy?
    
      - > Is item oversized?
    
      - > Is shipping expedited?

  - > If **YES to any** → Dropship

  - > If **NO to all** → In-House

### **2.1.4 Step 4: Order Placement**

#### **A. In-House Order**

  - > Select Standard Ground

  - > Use vendor default carrier

  - > Place order normally

  - > No FedEx account involved

**B. Dropship Order**

  - > Select FedEx shipping

  - > Enter FedEx account number in order notes

  - > Copy & paste customer shipping address

  - > Double-verify address formatting

  - > Place order

### **2.1.5 Step 5: Final Verification (MANDATORY)**

Before checkout, confirm:

  - > Item & quantity

  - > Stock status

  - > Shipping method

  - > FedEx account entered (if dropship)

  - > Customer address accuracy
