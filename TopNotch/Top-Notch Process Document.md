# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Critical Rules Summary:**

  - > TopNotch often ignores our shipping notes → charges higher. This is why most orders are processed in-house, not dropshipped

  - > Dropship ONLY if order ≥ $500 or meets other dropship criteria discussed in point 1.2

  - > Vendor ships from multiple warehouses

  - > Schlage C/R → in stock only

  - > Restricted Schlage keys → NEVER ship

  - > Oversized items → Marina approval mandatory

  - > Expedited → must confirm item is in-stock before ordering

  - > Standard ETA = 7 days

## **1.2 Dropship Eligibility & Criteria:**

Dropship is allowed ONLY if ANY of the following conditions are met:

  - > Order total ≥ $500 MOV

  - > Item cost \> $500

  - > Item is oversized or marked with additional shipping charges

  - > Customer requests expedited shipping (1–2 business days)

  - > Large quantity (high volume)

  - > Customer order is non-marketplace (NO Amazon/Walmart/eBay/Wayfair)

## **1.5 Substitutions:**

1.5.1 When Schlage C/R is Out-of-Stock, search alternatives in this sequence:

  - > **Security Lock**

  - > **Banner Solutions**

  - > **IML Security Supply**

These vendors often have equivalent Schlage SKUs.

### 1.5.2 Manufacturer Pricing Optimization:

If item belongs to any of these brands:

  - > **Assa Abloy**

  - > **Pemko**

  - > **Rockwood**

  - > **McKinney**

→ Transfer order to Assa Abloy (cheaper pricing).

**SECTION 2 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **2.1 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

#### **2.1.1 Step 1 — Verify Items**

  - > Ensure all items belong to TopNotch.

  - > Ensure no restricted Schlage keys.

  - > Confirm backorder eligibility.

#### **2.1.2 Step 2 — Price Verification**

  - > Match price with vendor site.

  - > If there is a slight difference (few cents to $1), acceptable.

#### **2.1.3 Step 3 — Add Items to Cart**

  - > Add all SKUs manually.

  - > Confirm cart total ≈ vendor total.

  - > Paste cart total into vendor notes.

#### **2.1.4 Step 4 — Check Stock**

  - > Green = In stock

  - > Yellow (“Available”) = Backorder

  - > Discontinued = Do not order

#### **2.1.5 Step 5 — Checkout**

  - > Shipping address dropdown → DK Hardware

  - > Comment box: “PLEASE SHIP FROM DIFFERENT WAREHOUSES**”**

#### **2.1.6 Step 6 — Payment**

  - > Payment = TND account (default)

  - > Enter PO number exactly

#### **2.1.7 Step 7 — Review**

  - > Double-check address, prices, quantities

#### **2.1.8 Step 8 — Place Order**

  - > Copy confirmation number → paste into vendor order system

# **2.2 PROCESSING A VENDOR ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **2.2.1 Step 1 — Customer Order Processing**

  - > Go to Process Incoming Order

  - > Assign items to TopNotch

  - > Navigate:  
    > TopNotch → Create New Order → Dropship

  - > Order Status:  
    > New Orders → Placed for Dropship

  - > Click Process Changes

  - > System generates Vendor Order \#

### **2.2.2 Step 2 — Customer Address Entry**

  - > Enter complete customer address

  - > Email: **purchasing@dkhardware.com**

  - > Phone: **305-851-2811**

### **2.2.3 Step 3 — Payment**

  - > Enter PO number

### **2.2.4 Step 4 — Final Steps**

  - > Review carefully

  - > Copy shipping address from confirmation → paste into vendor order

  - > Place order

  - > Copy green confirmation \# → paste into vendor order
