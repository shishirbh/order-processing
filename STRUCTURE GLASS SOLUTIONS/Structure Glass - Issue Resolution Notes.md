## Key vendor attributes that affect issue handling

- **Ships under our (DK) account?** No
- **Dropship allowed?** Yes
- **Can CS contact vendor directly?** _not specified_

## Which claim path applies

- **Shipping damage / loss in transit → Vendor Claim Sheet.** Because this vendor ships under **their own** account, add the order to the **vendor claim sheet** (Vendor RMA to be created by Claims dept; advise supplier we are looking for credit only).

## Standard actions on every issue (applies universally)

1. Create Issue in CRM and add the issue # as a hyperlink in the order notes.
2. Request images of the items and packaging from the customer.
3. For LTL Freight deliveries, also request a copy of the BOL and confirm whether the shipment was signed for and whether damage was noted to the driver.
4. Provide Vitaly with the replacement order number for approval before the replacement goes out.
5. If the customer wants a refund instead of replacement, advise Ksenia.
