## Sheet: SOP Problem Orders- Scenarios a

| Problem Type | Root Cause / Trigger | Action Plan & Resolution | Column 4 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Quote Requirements | Needs vendor quote and / or customer signature. | Action: Request exact quantity quote from vendor (mark as Urgent).    Customer Note: Explain "Special Order" terms: Non-returnable, non-cancellable, and non-refundable.  Resolution: | s |
| Discontinued Item | Item is no longer manufactured. | Action: Search for alternatives on the website or via vendor.    Resolution: Offer alternative to customer. If none exist, notify customer and raise cancellation. | s |
| Vendor Unavailable | Item listed but cannot be purchased from specific vendor ( E.g: Jeske ) | Action: Research alternative vendors or products.    Resolution: Offer the alternative solution to the customer. . If none exist, notify customer and raise cancellation. | s |
| Payment Discrepancy | Credit card details do not match billing info. | Action: Call the customer to re-verify details.    Resolution: Send the secure fraud link to successfully capture payment. | cs /s |
| PO Box Address | System flagged a PO Box for delivery. | Action: Call the customer immediately.    Resolution: Obtain a physical delivery address to proceed with the shipment. | cs/s |
| Regional Restriction | Item cannot be shipped to certain regions (e.g., California). | Action: Inform customer of regional restrictions.    Resolution: Check for an alternative shipping address within the approved regions; otherwise, cancel. | cs/s |
| Non-Purchasable | Fragile items (e.g., Laminates) prone to transit damage. | Action: Explain the high risk of damage to the customer.    Resolution: Inform customer we do not process these and raise cancellation. | cs/s |
| Freight Calculation | System failed to calculate accurate freight costs at checkout. | Action: Consult with Justin or Marina for accurate freight pricing.    Resolution: Inform the customer of the updated cost for approval. | cs/s |
| Core Fee | Items have an additional core deposit that is refunded when the core is returned to us | Action: Explain the core deposit to the customer and the refund process of that.   Resolution: Search "core fee" in the search bar in the order page, add the core fee and add the same quantity as the item. If item quantity is 2, then core fee quantity will be 2 | s |
| Hazmat | These items cannot be shipped outside USA | Action: Check with the customer if they have an US-Based address for shipping  Resolution: If they have an US address , update the shipping and process otherwise raise cancellation after explaing the customer | cs/s |
| Limited Quantity available | Items have a limited quantity available than what is ordered | Action: Contact the vendor (call/email) to confirm stock and update the customer.  Resolution: If available, obtain a quote with ETA and proceed. If partially available, confirm customer approval and proceed with available quantity. If unavailable or customer declines, process cancellation. | cs/s |
