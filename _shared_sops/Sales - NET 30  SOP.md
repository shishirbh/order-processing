**<span class="underline">SOP TO PROCESS NET30 REQUESTS, QUOTES & ORDERS</span>**

# **A. DETAILED WRITTEN SOP (CLEANED & EXPANDED)**

## **1. PURPOSE**

This SOP defines the standardized process for:

  - > Handling **Net 30 credit account requests**

  - > Processing **orders under approved Net 30 terms**

  - > Ensuring **credit verification, accurate PO validation, and correct order conversion**

  - > Minimizing **credit risk, processing errors, and order rework**

# **2. SCOPE**

This SOP applies to:

  - > All **customers requesting Net 30 terms**

  - > All **quotes converted to Net 30 orders**

  - > All **Net 30 repayment monitoring and follow-ups**

The **standard Quote SOP still applies** for:

  - > Quote creation

  - > Quote follow-up timelines

  - > Customer communication

This SOP only adds **Net 30–specific steps.**

# **3. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES**

## **Sales Representative**

Responsible for full lifecycle management of the Net 30 request.

Key responsibilities:

1.  > Identify customer eligibility

2.  > Send and collect Net 30 applications

3.  > Coordinate approval with **Accounts (Art Goldman – CFO)**

4.  > Communicate approval/rejection to customer

5.  > Verify Purchase Orders before order conversion

6.  > Convert quotes into Net 30 orders

7.  > Track delivery and follow up on repayment

8.  > Document all communication and actions

# **4. PROCESS PHASES**

**PHASE 1 — CUSTOMER TYPE IDENTIFICATION**

The first step is to determine **customer type and eligibility**.

### **Scenario 1 — B2B Customer**

If the customer is a **business customer (B2B):**

Action:

1.  > Send Net 30 credit application

2.  > Request completed form

3.  > Begin approval process

Order history is **not required** for B2B eligibility.

### **Scenario 2 — B2C Customer With No Order History**

If customer is **consumer (B2C)** and has **no order history**:

Action:

1.  > Inform customer that **at least one prepaid order is required**

2.  > Send Net 30 application in parallel

3.  > Net 30 review will occur **after prepaid order history exists**

Accepted prepaid methods:

  - > Credit Card

  - > ACH

  - > Wire Transfer

  - > Check

### **Important Rule**

**Net 30 customers are NOT eligible for discounts.**

Reason:  
Net terms already provide **extended payment advantages**.

Always decline requests such as:

  - > volume discounts

  - > coupon codes

  - > negotiated pricing

Unless **manager approval** is granted.

# **PHASE 2 — APPLICATION RECEIPT & SUBMISSION**

When the completed Net 30 application is received:

### **Step 1 — Verify Application**

Ensure the application contains:

  - > Company name

  - > Contact information

  - > Billing address

  - > Trade references

  - > Required signatures

If information is incomplete:

→ Request corrected application before submitting.

### **Step 2 — Inform Customer**

Send confirmation email.

Standard message:

> Net 30 approval typically takes **24–48 business hours**.

### **Step 3 — Submit for Approval**

Forward the application to:

**Art Goldman (CFO)  
**artgoldman@dkhardwaresupply.com

Include in the email:

  - > Completed application

  - > Quote number (if available)

  - > Customer details

### **Best Practice**

If possible:

  - > Collect product information

  - > Create a quote

  - > Include quote number in the approval request

Reason:

This allows the CFO to **assign an appropriate credit limit**.

# **PHASE 3 — CREDIT DECISION**

The Accounts team will either:

  - > **Approve**

  - > **Reject**

## **Scenario A — Application Rejected**

Actions:

1.  > Inform customer immediately

2.  > Offer prepaid payment options

Accepted payment methods:

  - > Credit card

  - > ACH / wire

  - > check

If the customer has **prior prepaid history**, advise:

> Net 30 eligibility may be reconsidered later.

Always document rejection in notes.

## **Scenario B — Application Approved**

Actions:

1.  > Inform customer of approval

2.  > Request **Accounts Payable contact (POC and Email)**

3.  > Proceed with quote creation (if not already created)

4.  > Send final quote

5.  > Request **Purchase Order (PO)**

# **PHASE 4 — PURCHASE ORDER (PO) VERIFICATION**

A Purchase Order **must exactly match the quote**.

Verify the following:

| **Field**       | **Requirement**  |
| --------------- | ---------------- |
| Item Numbers    | Must match quote |
| Quantities      | Must match quote |
| Unit Price      | Must match quote |
| Total Amount    | Must match quote |
| Shipping        | Must match quote |
| Bill To Address | Must match       |
| Ship To Address | Must match       |
| Customer Name   | Must match       |

## **If PO Does NOT Match Quote**

DO NOT convert the order.

Instead:

1.  > Contact customer

2.  > Request corrected PO

Alternative:

Supervisor approval required.

# **PHASE 6 — ORDER TRACKING**

The sales agent must monitor the order until delivery.

Provide customer:

  - > Shipment date

  - > Tracking number

  - > Estimated delivery date (ETA)

Continue monitoring until **delivery confirmation**.

# **PHASE 7 — PAYMENT FOLLOW-UP**

Net 30 repayment starts **30 days after delivery**.

Accepted payment methods:

  - > ACH

  - > Wire transfer

  - > Check

  - > Credit card payment link

### **Follow-up Frequency**

Minimum:

**Two follow-ups per week**

Until payment is received.

### **Contact Priority**

1.  > Accounts Payable team

2.  > Buyer

3.  > Company finance contact

### **Documentation Requirement**

Every follow-up must be recorded in notes.

Include:

  - > Date

  - > Contact method

  - > Response

  - > Expected payment date

# **5. QUALITY STANDARDS**

The following rules must always be followed:

1.  > Net 30 customers **cannot receive discounts**

2.  > Quote must match PO before order conversion

3.  > All actions must be documented

4.  > Do not paste screenshots into notes

5.  > Only paste **clear text information**

Never:

❌ Convert order without PO verification  
❌ Forget to upload PO document  
❌ Leave payment method as credit card  
❌ Apply discounts to Net 30 orders  
❌ Leave notes incomplete  
❌ Use screenshots in notes
