# **SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS**

### **1.1 Vendor Rules**

  - > Kelly is IMLSS Representative. We communicate only via email - <span class="underline">kelly.weiman@imlss.com</span>

  - > Minimum Order Value is $100 per warehouse for free shipping. This excludes freight charges.

  - > Out-of-stock items cannot be processed. Move such items to Out-of-Stock Queue.

  - > Items of 72" and over 72” must be dropshipped using our FedEx Account \# 632647611.

  - > Items valued above $100 must be dropshipped via vendor portal.

  - > Freight items must ship using a Bill of Lading.

  - > Freight items must order inhouse for international orders. First check the freight cost from vendor to DK Hardware warehouse and then the freight from our warehouse to the customer.

  - > Expedited orders (Next Day / Second Day) must use our FedEx account.

  - > We do not backorder any items — if unavailable, move the item to Out-of-Stock Orders.

  - > **Caution:** Never mix in-house and dropship items on a single PO. Process separately to avoid shipping conflicts.

  - > Average Shipping Time: 7–10 days

**Avoid:  
** ❌ Ordering out-of-stock items.  
❌ Mixing warehouses.  
❌ Using vendor UPS or third-party accounts.

### **1.2 Order Types**

**1.2.1 In-House Orders**

  - > These are items we stock, and we process through our own DKH warehouse.

  - > Orders are processed through the vendor website or by email.

**1.2.2 Dropship Orders**

Dropship is required when:

  - > Item is or over 72"

  - > Item value is above $100

  - > Vendor shipping method requires FEDEX account usage

  - > Freight shipment is required

  - > Customer requires direct delivery

### **Dropship via Vendor Portal**

When item value is above $100, process dropship through the vendor portal and apply an appropriate shipping method.

**SECTION 3 — PROCESSING ORDERS**

# **3.1 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — IN-HOUSE**

### **3.1.1 Step 1 —** Check availability on vendor portal.

### **3.1.2 Step 2 —** If in stock → process as in-house.

### **3.1.3 Step 3 —** If not in stock → Check Alternate Vendor for stock availability.

### **3.1.4 Step 4 —** If not in stock at the Alternate Vendor → move to Out-of-Stock Queue

### **3.1.5 Step 5 —** Email vendor using approved template. *Do not backorder.*

### **3.1.6 Step 6 — Template – In-House Email**

Hi Kelly,

Hope all is good. Please process this order and let us know if everything makes it to the order.

Thanks & Regards,

Purchasing Department

# **3.2 PROCESSING VENDOR ORDER — DROPSHIP**

### **3.2.1 Step 1 —** Create order on vendor portal.

### **3.2.2 Step 2 —** Select blind shipping = YES.

### **3.2.3 Step 3 —** Use **FedEx Account \# 632647611.**

### **3.2.4 Step 4 —** Add internal note to confirm with Marina if uncertain about dimensions or freight*.*

### **3.2.5 Step 5 —** Use “Invoice” as payment method.

### **3.2.6 Step 6 — Template –Dropship Email**

Hi,

Please ship directly to our customer.

SHIP CUSTOMER FEDEX GROUND USING OUR FEDEX ACCOUNT \#632647611

Kindly let us know if there are any changes.

Regards,

Purchasing Team
